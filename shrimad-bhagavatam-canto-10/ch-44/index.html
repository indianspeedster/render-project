Shrimad Bhagwatam - Book 1

Canto 10

Chapter 44

Kamsa slain by Krishna

श्रीमद्भागवतमहापुराणम्

दशमः स्कन्धः

कंसवधो नाम चतुश्चत्वारिंशः अध्यायः

(51 Shlokas)

श्री शुक उवाच

एवं चर्चितसङ्कल्पो भगवान् मधुसूदन ।

आससादाथ चाणूरं मुष्टिकं रोहिणीसुतः ॥10.44.1॥

Word-by-word meaning: श्री शुकः उवाच Shri Shuka said, एवम् in this way, चर्चित-सङ्कल्पः having firmly decided his mind, भगवान् Lord, मधुसूदनः Krishna the slayer of the asura Madhu, आससाद confronted, अथ then, चाणूरम् Chanura, मुष्टिकम् Mushtika, रोहिणी-सुतः Balarama the son of Rohini

Anvaya: श्री शुकः उवाच — एवं चर्चितसङ्कल्पः भगवान् मधुसूदनः अथ रोहिणीसुतः चाणूरं मुष्टिकं च आससाद।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: श्री शुकः उवाच Shri Shuka said, एवं in this way, चर्चितसङ्कल्पः having firmly decided his mind, भगवान् मधुसूदनः the Supreme Lord Krishna (the slayer of Madhu), अथ then, रोहिणीसुतः Balarama the son of Rohini, चाणूरं मुष्टिकं च Chanura and Mushtika, आससाद confronted.

Simple Meaning: Shri Shuka said: Having firmly fixed his mind in this way, the Supreme Lord Krishna confronted Chanura, while Balarama, the son of Rohini, stepped forward to face Mushtika.

हस्ताभ्यां हस्तयोर्बद्ध्वा पद्भ्यामेव च पादयोः ।

विचकर्षतुरन्योन्यं प्रसह्य विजिगीषया ॥10.44.2॥

Word-by-word meaning: हस्ताभ्याम् with their hands, हस्तयोः the hands, बद्ध्वा locking, पद्भ्याम् with their feet, एव indeed, च and, पादयोः the feet, विचकर्षतुः they pulled, अन्योन्यम् each other, प्रसह्य violently, विजिगीषया with a desire for victory

Anvaya: हस्ताभ्यां हस्तयोः पद्भ्याम् एव च पादयोः बद्ध्वा विजिगीषया अन्योन्यं प्रसह्य विचकर्षतुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: हस्ताभ्यां हस्तयोः Hands with hands, पद्भ्याम् एव च पादयोः and feet indeed with feet, बद्ध्वा locking, विजिगीषया with a desire for victory, अन्योन्यं each other, प्रसह्य violently, विचकर्षतुः they pulled.

Simple Meaning: Locking hand to hand and foot to foot, they violently pulled and wrestled with one another, each combatant intensely driven by the desire for victory.

अरत्नी द्वे अरत्निभ्यां जानुभ्यां चैव जानुनी ।

शिरः शीर्ष्णोरसोरस्तावन्योन्यमभिजघ्नतुः ॥10.44.3॥

Word-by-word meaning: अरत्नी forearms, द्वे both, अरत्निभ्याम् with their forearms, जानुभ्याम् with their knees, च and, एव indeed, जानुनी knees, शिरः head, शीर्ष्णा with the head, उरसा with the chest, उरः chest, तौ they two, अन्योन्यम् each other, अभिजघ्नतुः struck

अरत्नी (elbow/ sometimes the fist itself/forearm) अरत्निः ,  इकारान्तः, पुंलिङ्गः, घिसंज्ञकः, प्रथमाद्विवचनम् द्वितीयाद्विवचनम् in this case द्वितीयाद्विवचनम् src, Shabdapatha, Ashtadhyayi

Anvaya: तौ अरत्नी अरत्निभ्यां जानुनी जानुभ्यां च एव शिरः शीर्ष्णा उरः उरसा अन्योन्यम् अभिजघ्नतुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: तौ They two, अरत्नी अरत्निभ्यां forearm against forearm, जानुनी जानुभ्यां च एव and knee indeed against knee, शिरः शीर्ष्णा head against head, उरः उरसा chest against chest, अन्योन्यम् each other, अभिजघ्नतुः struck.

Simple Meaning: Forearm against forearm, knee against knee, head against head, and chest against chest, the two pairs of combatants struck each other furiously.

परिभ्रामणविक्षेपपरिरम्भावपातनैः ।

उत्सर्पणापसर्पणैश्चान्योन्यं प्रत्यरुन्धताम् ॥10.44.4॥

Word-by-word meaning: परिभ्रामण-विक्षेप-परिरम्भ-अवपातनैः by wheeling around, throwing, holding in a headlock, and slamming down, उत्सर्पण-अपसर्पणैः by moving forward and moving backward, च and, अन्योन्यम् each other, प्रत्यरुन्धताम् they countered

Anvaya: परिभ्रामणविक्षेपपरिरम्भावपातनैः उत्सर्पणापसर्पणैः च अन्योन्यं प्रत्यरुन्धताम्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: परिभ्रामणविक्षेपपरिरम्भावपातनैः By wheeling around, throwing, holding, and slamming down, उत्सर्पणापसर्पणैः च and by moving forward and backward, अन्योन्यं each other, प्रत्यरुन्धताम् they countered.

Simple Meaning: Spinning each other around, throwing, holding in tight locks, slamming one another down, and shifting positions by stepping forward and backward, the wrestlers constantly countered each other's maneuvers.

उत्थापनैरुन्नयनैश्चालनैः स्थापनैरपि ।

परस्परं जिगीषन्तावपचक्रतुरात्मनः ॥10.44.5॥

Word-by-word meaning: उत्थापनैः by lifting up, उन्नयनैः by carrying, चालनैः by pushing away, स्थापनैः by pinning down, अपि also, परस्परम् each other, जिगीषन्तौ desiring victory, अपचक्रतुः they did harm, आत्मनः to bodies

Anvaya: परस्परं जिगीषन्तौ उत्थापनैः उन्नयनैः चालनैः स्थापनैः अपि आत्मनः अपचक्रतुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: परस्परं Each other, जिगीषन्तौ desiring victory, उत्थापनैः उन्नयनैः by lifting up and carrying, चालनैः स्थापनैः अपि and also by pushing away and pinning down, आत्मनः to bodies, अपचक्रतुः they caused pain.

Simple Meaning: Desiring victory over one another, they lifted, carried, pushed, and pinned each other down, causing severe pain and strain to each other's bodies.

तद् बलाबलवद्युद्धं समेताः सर्वयोषितः ।

ऊचुः परस्परं राजन् सानुकम्पा वरूथशः ॥10.44.6॥

Word-by-word meaning: तत् that, बल-अबल-वत् between the strong and the weak, युद्धम् fight, समेताः assembled, सर्व-योषितः all the women, ऊचुः said, परस्परम् to one another, राजन् O King, स-अनुकम्पाः filled with pity, वरूथशः in groups

Anvaya: राजन् ! समेताः सर्वयोषितः तत् बलाबलवत् युद्धं (दृष्ट्वा) सानुकम्पाः वरूथशः परस्परम् ऊचुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: राजन् O King, समेताः assembled, सर्वयोषितः all the women, तत् बलाबलवत् युद्धं that fight between the strong and the weak, सानुकम्पाः filled with pity, वरूथशः in groups, परस्परम् to one another, ऊचुः spoke.

Simple Meaning: O King Parikshit, seeing that unequal match between the incredibly strong wrestlers and the tender boys, all the women assembled there were filled with pity. Gathering together in groups, they began to speak among themselves.

महानयं बताधर्म एषां राजसभासदाम् ।

ये बलाबलवद्युद्धं राज्ञोऽन्विच्छन्ति पश्यतः ॥10.44.7॥

Word-by-word meaning: महान् great, अयम् this, बत alas, अधर्मः irreligion, एषाम् of these, राज-सभा-सदाम् members of the royal assembly, ये who, बल-अबल-वत् between the strong and the weak, युद्धम् fight, राज्ञः of the king, अन्विच्छन्ति look on with approval, पश्यतः while he watches

Anvaya: बत ! एषां राजसभासदां महान् अयम् अधर्मः, ये पश्यतः राज्ञः (पुरतः) बलाबलवत् युद्धम् अन्विच्छन्ति।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: बत Alas, एषां राजसभासदां of these members of the royal assembly, महान् अयम् अधर्मः this is a great injustice, ये who, पश्यतः राज्ञः while the king watches, बलाबलवत् युद्धम् a fight between the strong and the weak, अन्विच्छन्ति look on and approve.

Simple Meaning: Alas! This is a great injustice being committed by the members of this royal assembly. Even as the king watches, they are willingly looking on and approving of this highly unequal match between the strong and the weak.

क्व वज्रसारसर्वाङ्गौ मल्लौ शैलेन्द्रसन्निभौ ।

क्व चातिसुकुमाराङ्गौ किशोरौ नाप्तयौवनौ ॥10.44.8॥

Word-by-word meaning: क्व where, वज्र-सार-सर्व-अङ्गौ whose entire bodies are as strong as a thunderbolt, मल्लौ the two wrestlers, शैल-इन्द्र-सन्निभौ resembling mighty mountains, क्व where, च and, अति-सुकुमार-अङ्गौ whose limbs are extremely tender, किशोरौ the two boys, न not, आप्त-यौवनौ having attained youth

Anvaya: वज्रसारसर्वाङ्गौ शैलेन्द्रसन्निभौ मल्लौ क्व, च अतिसुकुमाराङ्गौ न आप्तयौवनौ किशोरौ क्व !

Literal meaning of the anvaya: वज्रसारसर्वाङ्गौ Whose entire bodies are as strong as a thunderbolt, शैलेन्द्रसन्निभौ resembling mighty mountains, मल्लौ the two wrestlers, क्व where, च and, अतिसुकुमाराङ्गौ whose limbs are extremely tender, न आप्तयौवनौ not having attained youth, किशोरौ the two boys, क्व where!

Simple Meaning: Where are these two giant wrestlers, with bodies as hard as thunderbolts and large as mighty mountains, and where are these two tender young boys who have not even reached full youth? The match is completely unequal!

धर्मव्यतिक्रमो ह्यस्य समाजस्य ध्रुवं भवेत् ।

यत्राधर्मः समुत्तिष्ठेन्न स्थेयं तत्र कर्हिचित् ॥10.44.9॥

Word-by-word meaning: धर्म-व्यतिक्रमः violation of religious principles, हि indeed, अस्य of this, समाजस्य assembly, ध्रुवम् certainty, भवेत् must be, यत्र where, अधर्मः irreligion, समुत्तिष्ठेत् arises, न not, स्थेयम् one should stay, तत्र there, कर्हिचित् at any time

Anvaya: अस्य समाजस्य धर्मव्यतिक्रमः ध्रुवं भवेत् हि, यत्र अधर्मः समुत्तिष्ठेत् तत्र कर्हिचित् न स्थेयम्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: अस्य समाजस्य Of this assembly, धर्मव्यतिक्रमः a violation of religious principles, ध्रुवं भवेत् हि indeed certainly must happen, यत्र where, अधर्मः irreligion, समुत्तिष्ठेत् arises, तत्र there, कर्हिचित् at any time, न स्थेयम् one should not stay.

Simple Meaning: There is definitely a grave violation of religious principles taking place in this assembly. One should never remain at any time in a place where irreligion and injustice are rising.

न सभां प्रविशेत् प्राज्ञः सभ्यदोषाननुस्मरन् ।

अब्रुवन् विब्रुवन्नज्ञो नरः किल्विषमश्नुते ॥10.44.10॥

Word-by-word meaning: न not, सभाम् the assembly, प्रविशेत् should enter, प्राज्ञः a wise person, सभ्य-दोषान् the sins of the members of the assembly, अनुस्मरन् keeping in mind, अब्रुवन् not speaking, विब्रुवन् speaking falsely, अज्ञः or claiming ignorance, नरः a person, किल्विषम् sin, अश्नुते incurs

Anvaya: प्राज्ञः सभ्यदोषान् अनुस्मरन् सभां न प्रविशेत्, (यतः) अब्रुवन् विब्रुवन् अज्ञः (वा) नरः किल्विषम् अश्नुते।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: प्राज्ञः A wise person, सभ्यदोषान् अनुस्मरन् keeping in mind the sins of the assembly members, सभां न प्रविशेत् should not enter the assembly, अब्रुवन् by not speaking, विब्रुवन् by speaking falsely, अज्ञः or by claiming ignorance, नरः a person, किल्विषम् sin, अश्नुते incurs.

Simple Meaning: A wise person should not even enter an assembly if he knows its members are acting unjustly. By entering such a place and remaining silent, speaking falsely, or pretending not to know the truth, a person incurs direct shared responsibility for the sin.

वल्गतः शत्रुमभितः कृष्णस्य वदनाम्बुजम् ।

वीक्ष्यतां श्रमवार्युप्तं पद्मकोशमिवाम्बुभिः ॥10.44.11॥

Word-by-word meaning: वल्गतः of Him who is springing about, शत्रुम् the enemy, अभितः around, कृष्णस्य of Krishna, वदन-अम्बुजम् the lotus face, वीक्ष्यताम् just look at, श्रम-वारि-उप्तम् covered with drops of perspiration from effort, पद्म-कोशम् the bud of a lotus flower, इव like, अम्बुभिः with drops of water

Anvaya: शत्रुम् अभितः वल्गतः कृष्णस्य श्रमवार्युप्तं वदनाम्बुजम् अम्बुभिः पद्मकोशम् इव वीक्ष्यताम्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: शत्रुम् अभितः Around the enemy, वल्गतः of Him who is springing about, कृष्णस्य of Krishna, श्रमवार्युप्तं covered with drops of perspiration, वदनाम्बुजम् the lotus face, अम्बुभिः with drops of water, पद्मकोशम् इव like a lotus bud, वीक्ष्यताम् just look at.

Simple Meaning: Just look at the lotus face of Krishna as He springs around His enemy! Covered with drops of perspiration from His exertion, His face shines beautifully like a blooming lotus bud covered with drops of water.

किं न पश्यत रामस्य मुखमाताम्रलोचनम् ।

मुष्टिकं प्रति सामर्षं हाससंरम्भशोभितम् ॥10.44.12॥

Word-by-word meaning: किम् what, न not, पश्यत you all see, रामस्य of Balarama, मुखम् the face, आताम्र-लोचनम् with slightly copper-red eyes, मुष्टिकम् Mushtika, प्रति toward, स-अमर्षम् filled with anger, हास-संरम्भ-शोभितम् beautified by His smiling and furious rage

Anvaya: मुष्टिकं प्रति सामर्षम् आताम्रलोचनं हाससंरम्भशोभितं रामस्य मुखं किं न पश्यत !

Literal meaning of the anvaya: मुष्टिकं प्रति Toward Mushtika, सामर्षम् filled with anger, आताम्रलोचनं with slightly copper-red eyes, हाससंरम्भशोभितं beautified by His smiling and furious rage, रामस्य मुखं the face of Balarama, किं न पश्यत do you all not see!

Simple Meaning: Do you not see the face of Balarama as He confronts Mushtika? Filled with righteous anger, His eyes have turned slightly copper-red, and His face looks wonderfully beautiful, displaying a striking combination of a mocking smile and furious rage.

पुण्या बत व्रजभुवो यदयं नृलिङ्गगूढः पुराणपुरुषो वनचित्रमाल्यः ।

गाः पालयन् सहबलः क्वणयंश्च वेणुं विक्रीडयाञ्चति गिरित्ररमार्चिताङ्घ्रिः ॥10.44.13॥

Word-by-word meaning: पुण्याः auspicious, बत oh, व्रज-भुवः the lands of Vraja, यत् because, अयम् this, नृ-लिङ्ग-गूढः disguised in a human form, पुराण-पुरुषः the Primeval Person, वन-चित्र-माल्यः wearing colorful flower garlands of the forest, गाः the cows, पालयन् herding, सह-बलः together with Balarama, क्वणयन् च and playing, वेणुम् the flute, विक्रीडया with various pastimes, अञ्चति roams about, गिरित्र-रमा-अर्चित-अङ्घ्रिः whose feet are worshipped by the protector of mountains (Lord Shiva) and the goddess of fortune Lakshmi

Anvaya: बत! व्रजभुवः पुण्याः, यत् गिरित्ररमार्चिताङ्घ्रिः नृलिङ्गगूढः वनचित्रमाल्यः अयं पुराणपुरुषः सहबलः गाः पालयन् वेणुं क्वणयन् च विक्रीडया अञ्चति।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: बत Oh, व्रजभुवः the lands of Vraja, पुण्याः are supremely auspicious, यत् because, गिरित्ररमार्चिताङ्घ्रिः whose feet are worshipped by Lord Shiva and Lakshmi, नृलिङ्गगूढः disguised in a human form, वनचित्रमाल्यः wearing colorful forest flower garlands, अयं पुराणपुरुषः this Primeval Person, सहबलः together with Balarama, गाः पालयन् herding the cows, वेणुं क्वणयन् च and playing the flute, विक्रीडया अञ्चति roams about with various pastimes.

Simple Meaning: Oh, how supremely auspicious are the lands of Vraja! For it is there that the Primeval Person, whose feet are worshipped by Lord Shiva and Goddess Lakshmi, roams about in a disguised human form. Wearing colorful forest garlands, He herds the cows and plays His flute, filling the land with His divine pastimes alongside Balarama.

गोप्यस्तपः किमचरन् यदमुष्य रूपं लावण्यसारमसमोर्ध्वमनन्यसिद्धम् ।

दृग्भिः पिबन्त्यनुसवाभिनवं दुरापमेकान्तधाम यशसः श्रिय ऐश्वरस्य ॥10.44.14॥

Word-by-word meaning: गोप्यः the gopis, तपः austerities, किम् what, अचरन् performed, यत् because, अमुष्य of Him (Krishna), रूपम् the beauty, लावण्य-सारम् the essence of loveliness, असम-ऊर्ध्वम् unequaled and unsurpassed, अनन्य-सिद्धम् not achieved by any external means, दृग्भिः with their eyes, पिबन्ति they drink, अनुसव-अभिनवम् always fresh at every moment, दुरापम् difficult to obtain, एकान्त-धाम the exclusive abode, यशसः of fame, श्रियः of beauty, ऐश्वरस्य of opulence

Anvaya: गोप्यः किं तपः अचरन्, यत् (ताः) अमुष्य लावण्यसारम् असमोर्ध्वम् अनन्यसिद्धम् अनुसवाभिनवं दुरापं यशसः श्रियः ऐश्वरस्य एकान्तधाम रूपं दृग्भिः पिबन्ति।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: गोप्यः The gopis, किं तपः अचरन् what austerities did they perform, यत् because, अमुष्य of Him, लावण्यसारम् the essence of loveliness, असमोर्ध्वम् unequaled and unsurpassed, अनन्यसिद्धम् self-existent and natural, अनुसवाभिनवम् always fresh at every moment, दुरापं difficult to obtain, यशसः श्रियः ऐश्वरस्य of fame, beauty, and opulence, एकान्तधाम the exclusive abode, रूपं the beauty, दृग्भिः पिबन्ति they drink with their eyes.

Simple Meaning: What incredible austerities must the gopis have performed? With their eyes, they constantly drink in the beauty of Krishna, which is the very essence of loveliness, unequaled and unsurpassed. His beauty is self-existent, always fresh at every moment, difficult for others to attain, and the exclusive abode of all fame, beauty, and opulence.

या दोहनेऽवहनने मथनोपलेपप्रेङ्खेङ्खनार्भरुदितोक्षणमार्जनादौ ।

गायन्ति चैनमनुरक्तधियोऽश्रुकण्ठ्यो धन्या व्रजस्त्रिय उरुक्रमचित्तयानाः ॥10.44.15॥

Word-by-word meaning: याः who, दोहने during milking, अवहनने during threshing, मथन-उपलेप-प्रेङ्ख-ईङ्खन-अर्भ-रुदित-उक्षण-मार्जनादौ during churning, smearing floors, swinging on swings, comforting crying babies, sprinkling water, and cleaning, गायन्ति they sing, च and, एनम् about Him (Krishna), अनुरक्त-धियः with minds filled with deep attachment, अश्रु-कण्ठ्यः with throats choked with tears, धन्याः blessed, व्रज-स्त्रियः the women of Vraja, उरुक्रम-चित्त-यानाः whose minds are fully absorbed in Urukrama (the wide-striding Krishna)

Anvaya: याः दोहने अवहनने मथनोपलेपप्रेङ्खेङ्खनार्भरुदितोक्षणमार्जनादौ च अनुरक्तधियः अश्रुकण्ठ्यः एनं गायन्ति, (ताः) उरुक्रमचित्तयानाः व्रजस्त्रियः धन्याः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: याः Who, दोहने अवहनने during milking and threshing, मथनोपलेपप्रेङ्खेङ्खनार्भरुदितोक्षणमार्जनादौ च and during churning, smearing, swinging, comforting crying babies, sprinkling, and cleaning, अनुरक्तधियः with minds filled with deep attachment, अश्रुकण्ठ्यः with throats choked with tears, एनं गायन्ति sing about Him, (ताः) those, उरुक्रमचित्तयानाः whose minds are fully absorbed in Krishna, व्रजस्त्रियः women of Vraja, धन्याः are highly blessed.

Simple Meaning: The women of Vraja are truly blessed, for their minds are completely absorbed in Krishna. While carrying out their daily chores—whether milking cows, threshing grain, churning butter, smearing floors with cow dung, rocking swings, soothing crying babies, sprinkling water, or cleaning their homes—they constantly sing of Him with deep attachment, their throats choked with tears of pure love.

प्रातर्व्रजाद् व्रजत आविशतश्च सायं गोभिः समं क्वणयतोऽस्य निशम्य वेणुम् ।

निर्गम्य तूर्णमबला: पथि भूरिपुण्याः पश्यन्ति सस्मितमुखं सदयावलोकम् ॥10.44.16॥

Word-by-word meaning: प्रातः in the morning, व्रजात् from the village of Vraja, व्रजतः of Him who is leaving, आविशतः च and of Him who is entering, सायम् in the evening, गोभिः समम् together with the cows, क्वणयतः of Him who is playing, अस्य His, निशम्य hearing, वेणुम् the flute, निर्गम्य coming out, तूर्णम् quickly, अबलाः the women, पथि on the road, भूरि-पुण्याः possessing immense pious merit, पश्यन्ति they see, स-स्मित-मुखम् His smiling face, स-दया-अवलोकम् accompanied by His merciful glances

Anvaya: सायं प्रातः च गोभिः समं व्रजात् व्रजतः आविशतः च अस्य वेणुं क्वणयतः निशम्य, भूरिपुण्याः अबलाः तूर्णं निर्गम्य पथि सस्मितमुखं सदयावलोकं पश्यन्ति।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: सायं प्रातः च In the evening and morning, गोभिः समं together with the cows, व्रजात् व्रजतः leaving Vraja, आविशतः च and entering Vraja, अस्य His, वेणुं क्वणयतः playing of the flute, निशम्य hearing, भूरिपुण्याः possessing immense pious merit, अबलाः the women, तूर्णं निर्गम्य quickly coming out, पथि on the road, सस्मितमुखं His smiling face, सदयावलोकं accompanied by His merciful glance, पश्यन्ति they see.

Simple Meaning: When Krishna leaves Vraja in the morning with the cows and returns in the evening, the women of Vraja hear the sweet vibration of His flute. Possessing immense pious merit, they quickly run out onto the road to gaze upon His beautiful, smiling face, which is adorned with merciful glances.

एवं प्रभाषमाणासु स्त्रीषु योगेश्वरो हरिः ।

शत्रुं हन्तुं मनश्चक्रे भगवान् भरतर्षभ ॥10.44.17॥

Word-by-word meaning: एवम् in this way, प्रभाषमाणासु while they were speaking, स्त्रीषु the women, योग-ईश्वरः the Lord of all mystic power, हरिः Krishna, शत्रुम् the enemy, हन्तुम् to kill, मनः चक्रे fixed His mind, भगवान् the Supreme Personality of Godhead, भरत-ऋषभ O chief of the Bharatas (King Parikshit)

Anvaya: भरतर्षभ ! स्त्रीषु एवं प्रभाषमाणासु (सतीषु), योगेश्वरः भगवान् हरिः शत्रुं हन्तुं मनः चक्रे।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: भरतर्षभ O chief of the Bharatas, स्त्रीषु एवं प्रभाषमाणासु while the women were speaking in this way, योगेश्वरः भगवान् हरिः the Supreme Lord Hari, the master of all mystic power, शत्रुं हन्तुं to kill the enemy, मनः चक्रे fixed His mind.

Simple Meaning: O chief of the Bharatas, while the women of the assembly were speaking in this manner, the Supreme Lord Hari, the master of all mystic power, determined that it was now time to kill His opponent.

सभयाः स्त्रीगिरः श्रुत्वा पुत्रस्नेहशुचाऽऽतुरौ ।

पितरावन्वतप्येतां पुत्रयोरबुधौ बलम् ॥10.44.18॥

Word-by-word meaning: सभयाः filled with fear, स्त्री-गिरः the words of the women, श्रुत्वा hearing, पुत्र-स्नेह-शुचा-आतुरौ distressed by grief due to affection for their sons, पितरौ the parents, अन्वतप्येताम् suffered great anxiety, पुत्रयोः of their two sons, अबुधौ being unaware of, बलम् the strength

Anvaya: सभयाः स्त्रीगिरः श्रुत्वा पुत्रयोः बलम् अबुधौ पुत्रस्नेहशुचा आतुरौ पितरौ अन्वतप्येताम्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: सभयाः स्त्रीगिरः The fearful words of the women, श्रुत्वा hearing, पुत्रयोः बलम् of their two sons' strength, अबुधौ being unaware, पुत्रस्नेहशुचा आतुरौ distressed by grief due to affection for their sons, पितरौ the parents, अन्वतप्येताम् suffered great anxiety.

Simple Meaning: Hearing the anxious words of the women in the assembly, and being unaware of the true, almighty strength of their two sons, Vasudeva and Devaki were overwhelmed with grief out of parental affection and suffered intense anxiety for their safety.

तैस्तैर्नियुद्धविधिभिर्विविधैरच्युतेतरौ ।

युयुधाते यथान्योन्यं तथैव बलमुष्टिकौ ॥10.44.19॥

Word-by-word meaning: तैः तैः by those various, नियुद्ध-विधिभिः with the techniques of wrestling, विविधैः of different kinds, अच्युत-इतरौ Achyuta (Krishna) and the other (His opponent, Chanura), युयुधाते they fought, यथा just as, अन्योन्यम् each other, तथा एव in the exact same way, बल-मुष्टिकौ Balarama and Mushtika

Anvaya: अच्युतेतरौ तैः तैः विविधैः नियुद्धविधिभिः यथा अन्योन्यं युयुधाते तथा एव बलमुष्टिकौ।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: अच्युतेतरौ Achyuta and his opponent, तैः तैः विविधैः नियुद्धविधिभिः with those various, different kinds of wrestling techniques, यथा just as, अन्योन्यं each other, युयुधाते fought, तथा एव in the exact same way, बलमुष्टिकौ Balarama and Mushtika (fought).

Simple Meaning: Just as Krishna and His opponent Chanura fought each other using a vast array of different wrestling maneuvers and tactics, in the exact same manner, Balarama and Mushtika clashed against one another.

भगवद्गात्रनिष्पातैर्वज्रनिष्पेषनिष्ठुरैः ।

चाणूरो भज्यमानाङ्गो मुहुर्ग्लानिमवाप ह ॥10.44.20॥

Word-by-word meaning: भगवत्-गात्र-निष्पातैः by the blows from the limbs of the Supreme Lord, वज्र-निष्पेष-निष्ठुरैः which were as harsh and painful as the crashing of a thunderbolt, चाणूरः Chanura, भज्यमान-अङ्गः his limbs being broken, मुहुः repeatedly, ग्लानिम् exhaustion and fainting, अवाप attained, ह indeed

Anvaya: वज्रनिष्पेषनिष्ठुरैः भगवद्गात्रनिष्पातैः भज्यमानाङ्गः चाणूरः मुहुः ग्लानिम् अवाप ह।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: वज्रनिष्पेषनिष्ठुरैः Which were as harsh as the crashing of a thunderbolt, भगवद्गात्रनिष्पातैः by the blows from the limbs of the Supreme Lord, भज्यमानाङ्गः his limbs being broken, चाणूरः Chanura, मुहुः repeatedly, ग्लानिम् exhaustion, अवाप ह indeed attained.

Simple Meaning: Struck by the limbs of the Supreme Lord Krishna, which delivered blows as crushing and harsh as a thunderbolt, Chanura felt his bones fracturing and grew increasingly exhausted, repeatedly fainting on the battlefield.

स श्येनवेग उत्पत्य मुष्टीकृत्य करावुभौ ।

भगवन्तं वासुदेवं क्रुद्धो वक्षस्यबाधत ॥10.44.21॥

Word-by-word meaning: सः he (Chanura), श्येन-वेगः with the speed of a hawk, उत्पत्य springing up, मुष्टी-कृत्य clenching into fists, करौ hands, उभौ both, भगवन्तम् the Supreme Lord, वासुदेवम् Vasudeva (Krishna), क्रुद्धः furious, वक्षसि on the chest, अबाधत struck

Anvaya: सः क्रुद्धः श्येनवेगः उत्पत्य उभौ करौ मुष्टीकृत्य भगवन्तं वासुदेवं वक्षसि अबाधत।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: सः He, क्रुद्धः furious, श्येनवेगः with the speed of a hawk, उत्पत्य springing up, उभौ करौ both hands, मुष्टीकृत्य clenching into fists, भगवन्तं वासुदेवं the Supreme Lord Vasudeva, वक्षसि on the chest, अबाधत struck.

Simple Meaning: Furious and moving with the blinding speed of a hawk, Chanura sprang high into the air, clenched both his hands into fists, and struck the Supreme Lord Vasudeva directly on His chest.

नाचलत्तत्प्रहारेण मालाहत इव द्विपः ।

बाह्वोर्निगृह्य चाणूरं बहुशो भ्रामयन् हरिः ॥10.44.22॥

Word-by-word meaning: न अचलत् did not move or shake, तत्-प्रहारेण by that blow, माला-हतः struck by a flower garland, इव like, द्विपः an elephant, बाह्वोः by his two arms, निगृह्य seizing, चाणूरम् Chanura, बहुशः many times, भ्रामयन् whirling around, हरिः Lord Hari (Krishna)

Anvaya: मालाहतः द्विपः इव (हरिः) तत्प्रहारेण न अचलत्, (ततः) हरिः चाणूरं बाह्वोः निगृह्य बहुशः भ्रामयन्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: मालाहतः Struck by a garland of flowers, द्विपः इव like an elephant, (हरिः) Lord Hari, तत्प्रहारेण by that blow, न अचलत् did not shake, (ततः) then, हरिः Lord Hari, चाणूरं Chanura, बाह्वोः by his two arms, निगृह्य seizing, बहुशः many times, भ्रामयन् whirled him around.

Simple Meaning: Lord Krishna did not move even an inch from that powerful blow, remaining as unaffected as a mighty elephant struck by a light flower garland. Then, seizing Chanura firmly by his arms, Lord Hari whirled him around and around repeatedly.

भूपृष्ठे पोथयामास तरसा क्षीणजीवितम् ।

विस्रस्ताकल्पकेशस्रगिन्द्रध्वज इवापतत् ॥10.44.23॥

Word-by-word meaning: भू-पृष्ठे on the surface of the earth, पोथयामास slammed down, तरसा with great force, क्षीण-जीवितम् whose life was spent, विस्रस्त-आकल्प-केश-स्रक् with scattered ornaments, hair, and flower garlands, इन्द्र-ध्वजः the festival pole of King Indra, इव like, अपतत् he fell

Anvaya: तरसा भूपृष्ठे पोथयामास, (ततः) क्षीणजीवितः (सः) विस्रस्ताकल्पकेशस्रक् इन्द्रध्वजः इव अपतत्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: तरसा With great force, भूपृष्ठे on the ground, पोथयामास He slammed him down, (ततः) then, क्षीणजीवितः his life force spent, (सः) he, विस्रस्ताकल्पकेशस्रक् with scattered ornaments, hair, and garlands, इन्द्रध्वजः इव like the festival pole of Indra, अपतत् fell down.

Simple Meaning: Krishna slammed him down onto the ground with tremendous force. His life force completely spent, Chanura crashed to the earth like the giant ceremonial pole of King Indra, his ornaments, hair, and flower garlands scattered all around.

तथैव मुष्टिकः पूर्वं स्वमुष्ट्याभिहतेन वै ।

बलभद्रेण बलिना तलेनाभिहतो भृशम् ॥10.44.24॥

Word-by-word meaning: तथा एव in the exact same way, मुष्टिकः Mushtika, पूर्वम् previously, स्व-मुष्ट्या by his own fist, अभिहतेन who had been struck, वै indeed, बलभद्रेण by Balarama, बलिना the powerful, तलेन​ with the palm of the hand, अभिहतः slapped, भृशम् violently

Anvaya: तथा एव पूर्वं स्वमुष्ट्या अभिहतेन बलिना बलभद्रेण वै मुष्टिकः तलेन​ भृशम् अभिहतः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: तथा एव In the exact same way, पूर्वं previously, स्वमुष्ट्या by his own fist, अभिहतेन who had been struck, बलिना powerful, बलभद्रेण by Balarama, वै indeed, मुष्टिकः Mushtika, तलेन​ with the palm of His hand, भृशम् violently, अभिहतः was slapped.

Simple Meaning: In the exact same way, Mushtika—who had just moments before struck Balarama with his own fist—was now violently slapped with the open palm of the immensely powerful Lord Balarama.

प्रवेपितः स रुधिरमुद्वमन् मुखतोऽर्दितः ।

व्यसुः पपातोर्व्युपस्थे वाताहत इवाङ्घ्रिपः ॥10.44.25॥

Word-by-word meaning: प्रवेपितः trembling violently, सः he (Mushtika), रुधिरम् blood, उद्वमन् vomiting, मुखतः from his mouth, अर्दितः pained and tormented, व्यसुः lifeless, पपात fell down, उर्वी-उपस्थे on the lap of the earth, वात-आहतः struck by the wind, इव like, अङ्घ्रिपः a tree

Anvaya: अर्दितः प्रवेपितः मुखतः रुधिरम् उद्वमन् सः व्यसुः (सन्) वाताहतः अङ्घ्रिपः इव उर्व्युपस्थे पपात।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: अर्दितः Tormented, प्रवेपितः trembling violently, मुखतः रुधिरम् उद्वमन् vomiting blood from his mouth, सः he, व्यसुः (सन्) becoming lifeless, वाताहतः struck by the wind, अङ्घ्रिपः इव like a tree, उर्व्युपस्थे on the lap of the earth, पपात fell down.

Simple Meaning: Shattered by the blow and trembling violently, Mushtika began vomiting blood from his mouth. Completely lifeless, he crashed down upon the lap of the earth like a massive tree uprooted and toppled by a violent wind.

ततः कूटमनुप्राप्तं रामः प्रहरतां वरः ।

अवधील्लीलया राजन् सावज्ञं वाममुष्टिना ॥10.44.26॥

ददर्श चक्रायुधमग्रतो यतस्तदेव रूपं दुरवापमाप ॥10.44.39॥

Word-by-word meaning: सः he (Kamsa), नित्यदा constantly, उद्विग्न-धिया with an anxious or agitated mind, तम् that, ईश्वरम् Supreme Lord, पिबन् while drinking, वदन् while speaking, वा or, विचरन् while walking, स्वपन् while sleeping, श्वसन् while breathing, ददर्श saw, चक्र-आयुधम् the holder of the disc weapon (Krishna), अग्रतः in front of him, यतः wherever (he looked), तत् एव that very, रूपम् form, दुरवापम् difficult to attain, आप attained

Anvaya: सः नित्यदा उद्विग्नधिया पिबन् वदन् विचरन् स्वपन् श्वसन् वा अग्रतः चक्रायुधं तम् ईश्वरं ददर्श, तत् एव दुरवापं रूपम् आप।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: सः He, नित्यदा constantly, उद्विग्नधिया with an anxious mind, पिबन् while drinking, वदन् while speaking, विचरन् while walking, स्वपन् while sleeping, श्वसन् वा or while breathing, अग्रतः in front of him, चक्रायुधं the holder of the disc weapon, तम् ईश्वरं that Supreme Lord, ददर्श saw, तत् एव that very, दुरवापं difficult to attain, रूपम् form, आप attained.

Simple Meaning: With his mind constantly agitated by anxiety, Kamsa saw the Supreme Lord Krishna—the wielder of the disc—in front of him wherever he turned, whether he was drinking, speaking, walking, sleeping, or breathing. Due to this constant absorption, he ultimately attained that very same form of the Lord, which is otherwise exceedingly difficult to achieve.

तस्यानुजा भ्रातरोऽष्टौ कङ्कन्यग्रोधकादयः ।

अभ्यधावन्नभिक्रुद्धा भ्रातुर्निर्वेशकारिणः ॥10.44.40॥

Word-by-word meaning: तस्य His (Kamsa's), अनुजाः younger, भ्रातरः brothers, अष्टौ eight, कङ्क-न्यग्रोधक-आदयः headed by Kanka and Nyagrodhaka, अभ्यधावन् rushed forward, अभिक्रुद्धाः extremely angry, भ्रातुः of their brother, निर्वेश-कारिणः desired to pay their debts (by avenging him)

Anvaya: तस्य अष्टौ अनुजाः भ्रातरः कङ्कन्यग्रोधकादयः अभिक्रुद्धाः भ्रातुः निर्वेशकारिणः (सन्तः) अभ्यधावन्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: तस्य His, अष्टौ eight, अनुजाः भ्रातरः younger brothers, कङ्कन्यग्रोधकादयः headed by Kanka and Nyagrodhaka, अभिक्रुद्धाः extremely angry, भ्रातुः of their brother, निर्वेशकारिणः desiring to pay their debts, अभ्यधावन् rushed forward.

Simple Meaning: His eight younger brothers, headed by Kanka and Nyagrodhaka, became extremely angry and rushed forward to attack, desiring to pay back their debt to their brother by avenging his death.

तथातिरभसांस्तांस्तु संयत्तान् रोहिणीसुतः ।

अहन् परिघमुद्यम्य पशूनिव मृगाधिपः ॥10.44.41॥

Word-by-word meaning: तथा Thus, अति-रभसान् moving with extreme speed, तान् groups of them, तु indeed, संयत्तान् prepared for battle, रोहिणी-सुतः the son of Rohini (Balarama), अहन् killed, परिघम् an iron club, उद्यम्य lifting up, पशून् animals, इव just like, मृगाधिपः the king of beasts (a lion)

Anvaya: तथा रोहिणीसुतः उद्यम्य परिघं (गृहीत्वा) अतिरभसान् संयत्तान् तान् तु, मृगाधिपः पशून् इव अहन्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: तथा Thus, रोहिणीसुतः the son of Rohini, उद्यम्य lifting up, परिघं an iron club, अतिरभसान् moving with extreme speed, संयत्तान् prepared for battle, तान् तु indeed those (brothers), मृगाधिपः the king of beasts, पशून् इव just like animals, अहन् killed.

Simple Meaning: Lifting up his heavy iron club, Balarama, the son of Rohini, struck down those furious brothers who were rushing forward fully prepared for battle, just as a lion easily slaughters a herd of domestic animals.

नेदुर्दुन्दुभयो व्योम्नि ब्रह्मेशाद्या विभूतयः ।

पुष्पैः किरन्तस्तं प्रीताः शशंसुर्ननृतुः स्त्रियः ॥10.44.42॥

Word-by-word meaning: नेदुः Resounded, दुन्दुभयः kettledrums, व्योम्नि in the sky, ब्रह्म-ईश-आद्याः headed by Brahma and Shiva, विभूतयः powerful demigods, पुष्पैः with flowers, किरन्तः showering, तम् Him (Krishna), प्रीताः pleased, शशंसुः praised, ननृतुः danced, स्त्रियः the celestial women

Anvaya: व्योम्नि दुन्दुभयः नेदुः, ब्रह्मेशाद्याः विभूतयः प्रीताः (सन्तः) पुष्पैः तं किरन्तः शशंसुः, स्त्रियः (च) ननृतुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: व्योम्नि In the sky, दुन्दुभयः kettledrums, नेदुः resounded, ब्रह्मेशाद्याः headed by Brahma and Shiva, विभूतयः powerful demigods, प्रीताः pleased, पुष्पैः with flowers, तं Him, किरन्तः showering, शशंसुः praised, स्त्रियः the celestial women, ननृतुः danced.

Simple Meaning: Kettledrums resounded in the sky, and the powerful demigods, headed by Brahma and Shiva, showered flowers upon Krishna with great joy as they offered their praises. At the same time, the celestial women began to dance.

तेषां स्त्रियो महाराज सुहृन्मरणदुःखिताः।

तत्राभीयुर्विघ्नन्त्यः शीर्षाण्यश्रुविलोचनाः ॥10.44.43॥

Word-by-word meaning: तेषाम् Their (Kamsa and his brothers'), स्त्रियः wives, महाराज O great king (Parikshit), सुहृद्-मरण-दुःखिताः distressed by the death of their relatives, तत्र there, अभीयुः approached, विघ्नन्त्यः beating, शीर्षाणि their heads, अश्रु-विलोचनाः with eyes full of tears

Anvaya: महाराज ! तेषां सुहृन्मरणदुःखिताः अश्रुविलोचनाः स्त्रियः शीर्षाणि विघ्नन्त्यः तत्र अभीयुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: महाराज O great king, तेषां their, सुहृन्मरणदुःखिताः distressed by the death of their relatives, अश्रुविलोचनाः with eyes full of tears, स्त्रियः wives, शीर्षाणि their heads, विघ्नन्त्यः beating, तत्र there, अभीयुः approached.

Simple Meaning: O King Parikshit, the wives of Kamsa and his brothers, deeply distressed by the death of their husbands, approached the scene weeping profusely with eyes full of tears and beating their heads in deep grief.

शयानान् वीरशय्यायां पतीनालिङ्ग्य शोचतीः ।

विलेपुः सुस्वरं नार्यो विसृजन्त्यो मुहुः शुचः ॥10.44.44॥

Word-by-word meaning: शयानान् lying down, वीर-शय्यायाम् on the battlefield (the bed of heroes), पतीन् their husbands, आलिङ्ग्य embracing, शोचतीः lamenting, विलेपुः cried out loudly, सुस्वरम् piteously or with distinct voices, नार्यः the women, विसृजन्त्यः shedding, मुहुः repeatedly, शुचः tears of grief

Anvaya: वीरशय्यायां शयानान् पतीन् आलिङ्ग्य शोचतीः नार्यः मुहुः शुचः विसृजन्त्यः (सत्यः) सुस्वरं विलेपुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: वीरशय्यायां On the bed of heroes, शयानान् lying down, पतीन् their husbands, आलिङ्ग्य embracing, शोचतीः lamenting, नार्यः the women, मुहुः repeatedly, शुचः tears of grief, विसृजन्त्यः shedding, सुस्वरं piteously, विलेपुः cried out.

Simple Meaning: Embracing their husbands who lay dead on the battlefield—which served as their bed—the lamenting women wept piteously, repeatedly shedding floods of tears in their deep grief.

हा नाथ प्रिय धर्मज्ञ करुणानाथवत्सल ।

त्वया हतेन निहता वयं ते सगृहप्रजाः ॥10.44.45॥

Word-by-word meaning: हा Alas, नाथ O lord, प्रिय O dear one, धर्मज्ञ O knower of religious principles, करुण O merciful one, नाथ-वत्सल O protector of those who seek shelter, त्वया by you, हतेन who is killed, निहताः are killed, वयम् we, ते your, स-गृह-प्रजाः along with our homes and children

Anvaya: हा नाथ ! प्रिय ! धर्मज्ञ ! करुण ! नाथवत्सल ! त्वया हतेन (सता) ते सगृहप्रजाः वयं निहताः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: हा Alas, नाथ O lord, प्रिय O dear one, धर्मज्ञ O knower of religious principles, करुण O merciful one, नाथवत्सल O protector of those who seek shelter, त्वया by you, हतेन who is killed, ते your, सगृहप्रजाः along with our homes and children, वयं we, निहताः are killed.

Simple Meaning: Alas, O lord! O dear husband! O knower of righteousness! O merciful master who protects those who seek your shelter! Now that you have been killed, all of us, along with our homes and children, have also been destroyed.

त्वया विरहिता पत्या पुरीयं पुरुषर्षभ ।

न शोभते वयमिव निवृत्तोत्सवमङ्गला ॥10.44.46॥

Word-by-word meaning: त्वया By you, विरहिता bereft of, पत्या its lord, पुरी अयम् this city (Mathura), पुरुष-ऋषभ O chief among men, न not, शोभते appears beautiful, वयम् इव just like us, निवृत्त-उत्सव-मङ्गला stripped of its festivities and auspicious celebrations

Anvaya: पुरुषर्षभ ! पत्या त्वया विरहिता इयं पुरी, निवृत्तोत्सवमङ्गला वयम् इव न शोभते।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: पुरुषर्षभ O chief among men, पत्या its lord, त्वया by you, विरहिता bereft of, इयं पुरी this city, निवृत्तोत्सवमङ्गला stripped of its festivities and auspicious celebrations, वयम् इव just like us, न शोभते does not appear beautiful.

Simple Meaning: O chief among men, now that this city of Mathura is bereft of you, its lord, it has lost all its beauty. Deprived of all festivities and auspicious celebrations, it looks just as desolate as we do.

अनागसां त्वं भूतानां कृतवान् द्रोहमुल्बणम् ।

तेनेमां भो दशां नीतो भूतध्रुक् को लभेत शम्॥10.44.47॥

Word-by-word meaning: अनागसाम् of innocent, त्वम् you, भूतानाम् living beings, कृतवान् committed, द्रोहम् malice or persecution, उल्बणम् terrible, तेन by that, इमाम् this, भोः oh, दशाम् condition, नीतः brought to, भूत-ध्रुक् an injurer of living beings, कः who, लभेत can attain, शम् happiness

Anvaya: त्वम् अनागसां भूतानाम् उल्बणं द्रोहं कृतवान्, भोः ! तेन इमां दशां नीतः, (यतः) भूतध्रुक् कः शं लभेत?

Literal meaning of the anvaya: त्वम् You, अनागसां of innocent, भूतानाम् living beings, उल्बणं terrible, द्रोहं malice, कृतवान् committed, भोः oh, तेन by that, इमां दशां to this condition, नीतः are brought, भूतध्रुक् an injurer of living beings, कः who, शं happiness, लभेत can attain?

Simple Meaning: You committed terrible atrocities against innocent living beings, and oh lord, because of that you have been brought to this miserable state. Indeed, how can anyone who harms and oppresses living beings ever attain happiness?

सर्वेषामिह भूतानामेष हि प्रभवाप्ययः।

गोप्ता च तदवध्यायी न क्वचित् सुखमेधते ॥10.44.48॥

Word-by-word meaning: सर्वेषाम् Of all, इह in this world, भूतानाम् living beings, एषः this Person (Krishna), हि indeed, प्रभव-अप्ययः the source of creation and destruction, गोप्ता protector, च and, तत्-अवध्यायी one who slights or envies Him, न not, क्वचित् anywhere, सुखम् happiness, एधते prospers or enjoys

Anvaya: इह सर्वेषां भूतानां प्रभवाप्ययः गोप्ता च एषः हि, तदवध्यायी क्वचित् सुखं न एधते।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: इह In this world, सर्वेषां of all, भूतानां living beings, प्रभवाप्ययः the source of creation and destruction, गोप्ता protector, च and, एषः हि indeed this Person, तदवध्यायी one who envies Him, क्वचित् anywhere, सुखं happiness, न एधते does not enjoy.

Simple Meaning: This very Person is indeed the ultimate source of creation, destruction, and protection for all living beings in this world. One who insults or envies Him can never find happiness or prosper anywhere.

श्री शुक उवाच

राजयोषित आश्वास्य भगवाँल्लोकभावनः ।

यामाहुर्लौकिकीं संस्थां हतानां समकारयत् ॥10.44.49॥

Word-by-word meaning: श्री शुकः उवाच Shri Shuka said, राज-योषितः the royal women, आश्वास्य comforting, भगवान् the Supreme Lord, लोक-भावनः the creator and well-wisher of all living beings, याम् which, आहुः they prescribe, लौकिकीम् conventional or worldly, संस्थाम् funeral rites, हतानाम् for the deceased, समकारयत् caused to be performed properly

Anvaya: श्री शुकः उवाच - लोकभावनः भगवान् राजयोषितः आश्वास्य, हतानां यां लौकिकीं संस्थाम् आहुः (तां) समकारयत्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: श्री शुकः उवाच Shri Shuka said, लोकभावनः the creator and well-wisher of all living beings, भगवान् the Supreme Lord, राजयोषितः the royal women, आश्वास्य comforting, हतानां for the deceased, यां which, लौकिकीं संस्थाम् conventional funeral rites, आहुः they prescribe, समकारयत् caused to be performed properly.

Simple Meaning: Shri Shuka said: The Supreme Lord Krishna, who is the ultimate well-wisher and creator of all living beings, comforted the weeping royal women. He then arranged for the traditional funeral rites to be properly performed for all the deceased kings and brothers.

मातरं पितरं चैव मोचयित्वाथ बन्धनात् ।

कृष्णरामौ ववन्दाते शिरसाऽऽस्पृश्य पादयोः ॥10.44.50॥

Word-by-word meaning: मातरम् Their mother (Devaki), पितरम् their father (Vasudeva), च and, एव also, मोचयित्वा releasing, अथ then, बन्धनात् from shackles or captivity, कृष्ण-रामौ Krishna and Balarama, ववन्दाते offered their obeisances, शिरसा with their heads, आस्पृश्य touching, पादयोः the feet

Anvaya: अथ कृष्णरामौ मातरं पितरं च एव बन्धनात् मोचयित्वा, पादयोः शिरसा आस्पृश्य ववन्दाते।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: अथ Then, कृष्णरामौ Krishna and Balarama, मातरं their mother, पितरं च एव and also their father, बन्धनात् from captivity, मोचयित्वा releasing, पादयोः their feet, शिरसा with their heads, आस्पृश्य touching, ववन्दाते offered their obeisances.

Simple Meaning: Then, Krishna and Balarama released their mother Devaki and father Vasudeva from their shackles. Bowing down, They offered their respectful obeisances by touching Their parents' feet with Their heads.

देवकी वसुदेवश्च विज्ञाय जगदीश्वरौ ।

कृतसंवन्दनौ पुत्रौ सस्वजाते न शङ्कितौ ॥10.44.51॥

Word-by-word meaning: देवकी Devaki, वसुदेवः Vasudeva, च and, विज्ञाय understanding, जगत्-ईश्वरौ the two Lords of the universe, कृत-संवन्दनौ who had just offered their obeisances, पुत्रौ their two sons, सस्वजाते न did not embrace, शङ्कितौ filled with awe or hesitation

Anvaya: देवकी वसुदेवः च (तौ) जगदीश्वरौ विज्ञाय, कृतसंवन्दनौ पुत्रौ शङ्कितौ (सन्तौ) न सस्वजाते।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: देवकी Devaki, वसुदेवः च and Vasudeva, जगदीश्वरौ the two Lords of the universe, विज्ञाय understanding, कृतसंवन्दनौ who had just offered their obeisances, पुत्रौ their two sons, शङ्कितौ filled with awe, न सस्वजाते did not embrace.

Simple Meaning: Devaki and Vasudeva, now fully understanding that their two sons were actually the Lords of the universe, were filled with awe and hesitation. Consequently, they did not step forward to embrace them, even though the boys had just bowed down and offered their obeisances.

इति श्रीमद्भागवते महापुराणे पारमहंस्यां संहितायां दशमस्कन्धे पूर्वार्धे “कंसवधो नाम” चतुश्चत्वारिंशः अध्यायः ॥44॥

Thus ends the forty-fourth discourse named “Kamsa slain by Krishna” in the first half of Book Ten of the great and glorious Bhagavata-Purana.
