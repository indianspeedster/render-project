श्री शुक उवाच

गर्गः पुरोहितो राजन् यदूनां सुमहातपाः ।

व्रजं जगाम नन्दस्य वसुदेवप्रचोदितः ॥10.8.1॥

Word-by-word meaning: श्री शुकः उवाच Shri Shuka said, गर्गः Garga, पुरोहितः family priest, राजन् O king, यदूनाम् of the Yadus, सु-महा-तपाः one of great austerity, व्रजम् to Vraja, जगाम went, नन्दस्य of Nanda, वसुदेव-प्रचोदितः prompted by Vasudeva

Anvaya: श्री शुकः उवाच — राजन्! यदूनां पुरोहितः सुमहातपाः गर्गः वसुदेवप्रचोदितः नन्दस्य व्रजं जगाम।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: श्री शुकः उवाच Shri Shuka said — राजन् O king, यदूनां पुरोहितः the family priest of the Yadus, सुमहातपाः one of great austerity, गर्गः Garga, वसुदेवप्रचोदितः prompted by Vasudeva, नन्दस्य व्रजं जगाम went to Nanda's Vraja.

Simple meaning: Shri Shuka said: O king! Garga Muni, the family priest of the Yadus and a sage of great austerity, prompted by Vasudeva, went to Nanda's Vraja.

तं दृष्ट्वा परमप्रीतः प्रत्युत्थाय कृताञ्जलिः ।

आनर्चाधोक्षजधिया प्रणिपातपुरःसरम् ॥10.8.2॥

Word-by-word meaning: तम् him, दृष्ट्वा having seen, परम-प्रीतः extremely pleased, प्रत्युत्थाय rising up, कृत-अञ्जलिः with folded hands, आनर्च worshiped, अधोक्षज-धिया considering him to be Lord Adhokshaja (Vishnu) himself, प्रणिपात-पुरःसरम् by first offering prostrations

Anvaya: तं दृष्ट्वा परमप्रीतः प्रत्युत्थाय कृताञ्जलिः प्रणिपातपुरःसरम् अधोक्षजधिया आनर्च।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: तं दृष्ट्वा Having seen him, परमप्रीतः extremely pleased, प्रत्युत्थाय rising up, कृताञ्जलिः with folded hands, प्रणिपातपुरःसरम् preceded by prostrations, अधोक्षजधिया with the understanding that he represents Adhokshaja, आनर्च worshiped.

Simple meaning: Having seen Garga Muni, Nanda was extremely pleased. Rising up with folded hands and first offering prostrations, he worshiped him with the understanding that the sage represents Lord Adhokshaja (the Supreme Being beyond sense perception).

सूपविष्टं कृतातिथ्यं गिरा सूनृतया मुनिम् ।

नन्दयित्वाब्रवीद् ब्रह्मन् पूर्णस्य करवाम किम् ॥10.8.3॥

Word-by-word meaning: सु-उपविष्टम् comfortably seated, कृत-आतिथ्यम् having shown hospitality, गिरा with words, सूनृतया sweet and truthful, मुनिम् the sage, नन्दयित्वा having pleased, अब्रवीत् said, ब्रह्मन् O holy one, पूर्णस्य for the fulfilled one, करवाम may we do, किम् what

Anvaya: सूपविष्टं कृतातिथ्यं मुनिं सूनृतया गिरा नन्दयित्वा अब्रवीत् –  ब्रह्मन्! पूर्णस्य किं करवाम?

Literal meaning of the anvaya: सूपविष्टं कृतातिथ्यं Having seated him well and shown hospitality, मुनिं सूनृतया गिरा नन्दयित्वा having pleased the sage with sweet and truthful words, अब्रवीत् said, ब्रह्मन् पूर्णस्य किं करवाम O holy one, what may we do for you who are already fulfilled?

Simple meaning: Having seated the sage comfortably, shown him hospitality, and pleased him with sweet and truthful words, Nanda said: "O holy one! What can we do for you who are already self-fulfilled?"

महद्विचलनं नृणां गृहिणां दीनचेतसाम् ।

निःश्रेयसाय भगवन् कल्पते नान्यथा क्वचित् ॥10.8.4॥

Word-by-word meaning: महत्-विचलनम् movement of great souls, नृणाम् of men, गृहिणाम् householders, दीन-चेतसाम् of distressed minds, निःश्रेयसाय for ultimate good, भगवन् O respected one, कल्पते serves, न not, अन्यथा otherwise, क्वचित् anywhere

Anvaya: भगवन्! दीनचेतसां गृहिणां नृणां महद्विचलनं निःश्रेयसाय कल्पते, क्वचित् अन्यथा न।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: भगवन् O respected one! दीनचेतसां गृहिणां नृणाम् of householders with distressed minds, महद्विचलनम् the movement/visit of great souls, निःश्रेयसाय कल्पते serves for the ultimate good, क्वचित् अन्यथा न not otherwise anywhere.

Simple meaning: O respected one! The visit of great souls like you serves for the ultimate good of householders whose minds are distressed by worldly concerns. It is never otherwise.

ज्योतिषामयनं साक्षाद् यत्तज्ज्ञानमतीन्द्रियम् ।

प्रणीतं भवता येन पुमान् वेद परावरम् ॥10.8.5॥

Word-by-word meaning: ज्योतिषाम् of luminaries, अयनम् movements, साक्षात् directly, यत् which, तत् that, ज्ञानम् knowledge, अति-इन्द्रियम् transcendental to senses, प्रणीतम् propounded, भवता by you, येन by which, पुमान् a person, वेद knows, पर-अवरम् past and future

परावरम्  परावरम् वि. पर-अवरम् - पहले का और बाद का या आगामी or  दूर और निकट src. Kosha Ashtadhyayi

Anvaya: यत् ज्ञानम् अतीन्द्रियं, येन पुमान् परावरं वेद, तत्  ज्योतिषाम् अयनं साक्षात् भवता प्रणीतम्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: यत् ज्ञानम् अतीन्द्रियं The knowledge which is beyond the perception of senses, येन पुमान् परावरं वेद by which a person can know about past and future, तत् ज्योतिषाम् अयनं that movement (path) of the stars (astrology), साक्षात् भवता प्रणीतम् has been propounded by you yourself.

Simple meaning: You have directly propounded that transcendental knowledge of the movements of celestial luminaries, by which a person can understand both past and the future.

त्वं हि ब्रह्मविदां श्रेष्ठः संस्कारान् कर्तुमर्हसि ।

बालयोरनयोर्नृणां जन्मना ब्राह्मणो गुरुः ॥10.8.6॥

Word-by-word meaning: त्वम् you, हि indeed, ब्रह्म-विदाम् of knowers of Brahman, श्रेष्ठः best, संस्कारान् sacraments, कर्तुम् to perform, अर्हसि are worthy, बालयोः of the two boys, अनयोः of these, नृणाम् of people, जन्मना by birth, ब्राह्मणः a brahmin, गुरुः teacher

Anvaya: त्वं हि ब्रह्मविदां श्रेष्ठः। अनयोः बालयोः संस्कारान् कर्तुम् अर्हसि। ब्राह्मणः जन्मना नृणां गुरुः (अस्ति)।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: त्वं हि ब्रह्मविदां श्रेष्ठः You indeed are the best among knowers of Brahman. अनयोः बालयोः संस्कारान् कर्तुम् अर्हसि You are worthy to perform the sacraments for these two boys. ब्राह्मणः जन्मना नृणां गुरुः (अस्ति) A brahmin by birth is the spiritual teacher of people.

Simple meaning: You are indeed the best among the knowers of Brahman. You are worthy to perform the purificatory sacraments for these two boys, for a brahmin by birth is the natural spiritual teacher of all people.

गर्ग उवाच

यदूनामहमाचार्यः ख्यातश्च भुवि सर्वतः ।

सुतं मया संस्कृतं ते मन्यते देवकीसुतम् ॥10.8.7॥

Word-by-word meaning: गर्गः उवाच Garga said, यदूनाम् of the Yadus, अहम् I, आचार्यः spiritual teacher, ख्यातः known, च and, भुवि in the world, सर्वतः everywhere, सुतम् son, मया by me, संस्कृतम् sanctified, ते your, मन्यते will think, देवकी-सुतम् son of Devaki

Anvaya: गर्गः उवाच — अहं यदूनाम् आचार्यः, भुवि सर्वतः च ख्यातः (अस्मि)। मया संस्कृतं ते सुतं देवकीसुतं मन्यते।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: गर्गः उवाच Garga said — अहं यदूनाम् आचार्यः I am the spiritual teacher of the Yadus, भुवि सर्वतः च ख्यातः (अस्मि) and known everywhere in the world. मया संस्कृतं ते सुतं Your son sanctified by me, देवकीसुतं मन्यते will be thought to be the son of Devaki.

Simple meaning: Garga Muni said: I am the spiritual teacher of the Yadus and am known everywhere in the world as such. If I perform the sacraments for your son, people will suspect that He is actually the son of Devaki.

कंसः पापमतिः सख्यं तव चानकदुन्दुभेः ।

देवक्या अष्टमो गर्भो न स्त्री भवितुमर्हति ॥10.8.8॥

Word-by-word meaning: कंसः Kamsa, पाप-मतिः evil minded, सख्यम् friendship, तव your, च and, आनकदुन्दुभेः of Anakadundubhi (Vasudeva), देवक्याः of Devaki, अष्टमः eighth, गर्भः pregnancy, न not, स्त्री female, भवितुम् to be, अर्हति is possible

Anvaya: कंसः पापमतिः (अस्ति)। तव च आनकदुन्दुभेः सख्यं (अस्ति)। देवक्याः अष्टमः गर्भः स्त्री भवितुं न अर्हति।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: कंसः पापमतिः (अस्ति) Kamsa is evil minded. तव च आनकदुन्दुभेः सख्यम् (अस्ति) There is friendship between you and Anakadundubhi (Vasudeva). देवक्याः अष्टमः गर्भः (Kamsa thinks that) the eighth pregnancy of Devaki, स्त्री भवितुं न अर्हति is not possible to be a female.

Simple meaning: Kamsa is evil minded and knows of your friendship with Vasudeva (Anakadundubhi). He thinks that the eighth child of Devaki could not possibly be a girl.

इति सञ्चिन्तयञ्छ्रुत्वा देवक्या दारिकावचः ।

अपि हन्ताऽऽगताशङ्कस्तर्हि तन्नोऽनयो भवेत् ॥10.8.9॥

Word-by-word meaning: इति thus, सञ्चिन्तयन् pondering, श्रुत्वा having heard, देवक्याः of Devaki, दारिका-वचः words of the girl, अपि even, हन्ता-आगत-आशङ्कः suspicious of killer having come, तर्हि then, तत् that, नः our, अनयः misfortune, भवेत् may become

Anvaya: इति सञ्चिन्तयन्, अपि देवक्याः दारिकावचः श्रुत्वा हन्तागताशङ्कः, तर्हि तत् नः अनयः भवेत्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: इति सञ्चिन्तयन् Thus pondering, अपि देवक्याः दारिकावचः श्रुत्वा also, having heard the words of the girl born to Devaki, हन्तागताशङ्कः suspicious of killer having come, तर्हि then, तत् नः अनयः भवेत् that may become our misfortune.

Simple meaning: Thus pondering ( that the eight child cannot be a girl), and also having heard the words spoken by the girl born to Devaki, Kamsa's suspicion – that his killer has arrived – will surely be aroused. Then that could become a cause of misfortune for us.

नन्द उवाच

अलक्षितोऽस्मिन् रहसि मामकैरपि गोव्रजे ।

कुरु द्विजातिसंस्कारं स्वस्तिवाचनपूर्वकम् ॥10.8.10॥

Word-by-word meaning: नन्दः उवाच Nanda said, अलक्षितः unnoticed, अस्मिन् in this, रहसि secret, मामकैः by my people, अपि even, गोव्रजे in the cowherd settlement, कुरु perform, द्विजाति-संस्कारम् rites appropriate by a member of the twice-born class (brahmin), स्वस्ति-वाचन-पूर्वकम् preceded by benedictory recitations

Anvaya: नन्दः उवाच — अस्मिन् गोव्रजे रहसि मामकैः अपि अलक्षितः स्वस्तिवाचनपूर्वकं द्विजातिसंस्कारं कुरु।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: नन्दः उवाच Nanda said — अस्मिन् गोव्रजे रहसि In this cowherd settlement, in secret, मामकैः अपि अलक्षितः unnoticed even by my own people, स्वस्तिवाचनपूर्वकं preceded by benedictory recitations, द्विजातिसंस्कारं कुरु perform rites appropriate by a member of the twice-born class (brahmin).

Simple meaning: Nanda said: Please perform the chanting of benedictory mantras and rites appropriate by a member of the twice-born class (brahmin) for the boys, in this cowshed in secret, unnoticed even by my own people.

श्री शुक उवाच

एवं सम्प्रार्थितो विप्रः स्वचिकीर्षितमेव तत् ।

चकार नामकरणं गूढो रहसि बालयोः ॥10.8.11॥

Word-by-word meaning: श्री शुकः उवाच Shri Shuka said, एवम् thus, सम्प्रार्थितः requested, विप्रः the brahmin, स्व-चिकीर्षितम् his own desire, एव indeed, तत् that, चकार performed, नाम-करणम् name giving ceremony, गूढः secretly, रहसि in privacy, बालयोः of the two boys

Anvaya: श्री शुकः उवाच — एवं सम्प्रार्थितः विप्रः तत् स्वचिकीर्षितम् एव गूढः रहसि बालयोः नामकरणं चकार।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: श्री शुकः उवाच Shri Shuka said — एवं सम्प्रार्थितः Thus requested, विप्रः the brahmin, तत् स्वचिकीर्षितम् एव which was indeed his own desire, गूढः रहसि secretly in privacy, बालयोः of the two boys, नामकरणं चकार performed the name giving ceremony.

Simple meaning: Shri Shuka said: Thus requested, the brahmin performed the name-giving ceremony for the two boys in secret privacy, which was indeed what he himself had desired to do.

गर्ग उवाच

अयं हि रोहिणीपुत्रो रमयन् सुहृदो गुणैः ।

आख्यास्यते राम इति बलाधिक्याद् बलं विदुः ।

यदूनामपृथग्भावात् सङ्कर्षणमुशन्त्युत ॥10.8.12॥

Word-by-word meaning: गर्गः उवाच Garga said, अयम् this, हि indeed, रोहिणी-पुत्रः son of Rohini, रमयन् delighting, सुहृदः friends, गुणैः by qualities, आख्यास्यते will be called, रामः Rama, इति thus, बल-आधिक्यात् due to excess of strength, बलम् Bala, विदुः will be known (by everybody) as, यदूनाम् of the Yadus, अपृथक्-भावात् because of non separation, सङ्कर्षणम् Sankarshana, उशन्ति call, उत also

विदुः विद् , कर्तरि लट्लकारः (परस्मैपदम्) , प्रथमपुरुषः , बहुवचनम्  src. Dhatupatha. Ashtadhyayi

उशन्ति वश् वशँ कान्तौ अदादिः परस्मैपदी सकर्मकः सेट् (to wish, to long for, to desire) (इच्छा करना, चाहना) कर्तरि लट्लकारः (परस्मैपदम्) प्रथमपुरुषः बहुवचनम् (उशन्ति) src. Dhatupatha. Ashtadhyayi

Anvaya: गर्गः उवाच — अयं हि रोहिणीपुत्रः गुणैः सुहृदः रमयन् रामः इति आख्यास्यते। बलाधिक्यात् बलम् विदुः। यदूनाम् अपृथग्भावात् सङ्कर्षणम् उत उशन्ति।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: गर्गः उवाच Garga said — अयं हि रोहिणीपुत्रः This son of Rohini indeed, गुणैः सुहृदः रमयन् delighting friends by His qualities, रामः इति आख्यास्यते will be called Rama. बलाधिक्यात् बलम् विदुः due to His excess of strength everyone will know Him as Bala. यदूनाम् अपृथग्भावात् because of His inseparability from the Yadus, सङ्कर्षणम् उत उशन्ति they will also call Him Sankarshana.

Simple meaning: Garga said: This son of Rohini, who will delight His friends with His virtuous qualities, will be called Rama. The wise will know Him as Bala due to His extraordinary strength. And because of His inseparable connection with the Yadu dynasty, He will also be called Sankarshana.

आसन् वर्णास्त्रयो ह्यस्य गृह्णतोऽनुयुगं तनूः ।

शुक्लो रक्तस्तथा पीत इदानीं कृष्णतां गतः ॥10.8.13॥

Word-by-word meaning: आसन् were, वर्णाः colors, त्रयः three, हि indeed, अस्य His, गृह्णतः assuming, अनुयुगम् age after age, तनूः bodies, शुक्लः white, रक्तः red, तथा also, पीतः yellow, इदानीम् now, कृष्णताम् blackness, गतः attained

Anvaya: अस्य अनुयुगं तनूः गृह्णतः हि त्रयः वर्णाः आसन्, शुक्लः रक्तः तथा पीतः, इदानीं कृष्णतां गतः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: क्वचित् असमये वत्सान् मुञ्चन् क्रोशसंजातहासः Sometimes releasing the calves at the wrong time, laughs when scolded. कल्पितैः स्तेययोगैः स्तेयं स्वादु दधि पयः अत्ति By devised means of stealing He steals and eats sweet yogurt and milk. अथ मर्कान् भोक्ष्यन् विभजति Then, He feeds and distributes to monkeys. सः चेत् न अत्ति If He does not eat, भाण्डं भिनत्ति He breaks the pot. द्रव्यालाभे When things are not available, सः गृहकुपितः तोकान् उपक्रोश्य याति He becomes angry at the house, scolds the children and goes away.

Simple meaning: Sometimes He releases the calves at the wrong time and laughs when He is scolded. By devising clever means of stealing, He steals and eats sweet yogurt and milk. Then He feeds and shares it with monkeys. If He doesn't eat, He breaks the pots. When things are not available in a house, He becomes angry at that house, scolds the children there, and goes away.

हस्ताग्राह्ये रचयति विधिं पीठकोलूखलाद्यैश्छिद्रं ह्यन्तर्निहितवयुनः शिक्यभाण्डेषु तद्वित् ।

ध्वान्तागारे धृतमणिगणं स्वाङ्गमर्थप्रदीपं काले गोप्यो यर्हि गृहकृत्येषु सुव्यग्रचित्ताः ॥10.8.30॥

Literal meaning of the anvaya: यर्हि काले गोप्यः गृहकृत्येषु सुव्यग्रचित्ताः When at a time the gopis have very busy minds with household duties, हस्ताग्राह्ये when it is out of reach of His hands, अन्तर्निहितवयुनः He who has the knowledge, पीठकोलूखलाद्यैः विधिं रचयति arranges a method with stools, mortars etc., शिक्यभाण्डेषु inside the hanging pots and vessels, तद्वित् छिद्रं हि (करोति) knowing that, makes a hole. ध्वान्तागारे In the dark room, धृतमणिगणं स्वाङ्गम् अर्थप्रदीपम् (भवति) His own body wearing gems serves as a lamp.

Simple meaning: When the gopis are very busy with household duties, knowing that yogurt and butter are kept in hanging pots out of reach, He arranges a method using stools, mortars and such things to reach them and makes a hole in the vessels. In the dark room, His own body adorned with shining gems serves as a lamp.

इत्थं स्त्रीभिः सभयनयनश्रीमुखालोकिनीभिर्व्याख्यातार्था प्रहसितमुखी न ह्युपालब्धुमैच्छत् ॥10.8.31॥

Literal meaning of the anvaya: एवम् उशति वास्तौ धार्ष्ट्यानि मेहनादीनि कुरुते Thus in clean places in the house, He performs audacious acts like urinating. स्तेयोपायैः विरचितकृतिः सुप्रतीकः यथा आस्ते Stealing with by coming up with various ways, He is sitting like a good boy. इत्थं सभयनयनश्रीमुखालोकिनीभिः स्त्रीभिः Thus by women who watched the charming face (of ShriKrishna) with terrified eyes, व्याख्यातार्था प्रहसितमुखी Yashoda who was apprised of the situation, with a smiling face, न हि उपालब्धुम् ऐच्छत् did not, indeed, desire to reproach.

Simple meaning: He urinates in our clean houses and does other such mischievous acts. And having accomplished his work through pilfering devices, he sits (by your side) as a very innocent boy." Yashoda, to whom the matter was thus reported at length by the (cowherd) women—who watched the charming countenance (of ShriKrishna) with terrified eyes—did not feel inclined (even) to rebuke her son and stood with a laughing face.

Simple meaning: May we two, when born in this world, have supreme devotion to the great Lord Hari, the supreme controller of the universe, by which devotion one can easily cross over all misfortune in this world.

अस्त्वित्युक्तः स भगवान् व्रजे द्रोणो महायशाः ।

जज्ञे नन्द इति ख्यातो यशोदा सा धराभवत् ॥10.8.50॥

Word-by-word meaning: अस्तु so be it, इति thus, उक्तः told, सः he, भगवान् worshipful, व्रजे in Vraja, द्रोणः Drona, महा-यशाः of great fame, जज्ञे was born, नन्दः Nanda, इति thus, ख्यातः known, यशोदा Yashoda, सा she, धरा Dhara, अभवत् became

Anvaya: अस्तु – इति उक्तः महायशाः सः भगवान् द्रोणः व्रजे नन्दः इति ख्यातः जज्ञे, सा धरा यशोदा अभवत्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: अस्तु इति उक्तः Being told "so be it", महायशाः सः भगवान् द्रोणः that worshipful and greatly renowned Drona, व्रजे in Vraja, नन्दः इति ख्यातः जज्ञे was born known as Nanda, सा धरा that Dhara, यशोदा अभवत् became Yashoda.

Simple meaning: Being blessed with "so be it" by Lord Brahma, that worshipful and greatly renowned Drona was born in Vraja and became known as Nanda. And Dhara became Yashoda.

ततो भक्तिर्भगवति पुत्रीभूते जनार्दने ।

दम्पत्योर्नितरामासीद् गोपगोपीषु भारत ॥10.8.51॥

Word-by-word meaning: ततः then, भक्तिः devotion, भगवति in the Supreme Lord, पुत्री-भूते who had become their son, जनार्दने in Janardana, दम्पत्योः of the couple, नितराम् extremely, आसीत् was, गोप-गोपीषु among cowherds and gopis, भारत O descendant of Bharata

Anvaya: भारत! ततः भगवति जनार्दने पुत्रीभूते गोपगोपीषु दम्पत्योः नितरां भक्तिः आसीत्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: भारत O descendant of Bharata!, ततः भगवति जनार्दने पुत्रीभूते Then when the Supreme Lord Janardana become their son, गोपगोपीषु among the cowherds and gopis, दम्पत्योः of the couple, नितरां भक्तिः आसीत् there was intense devotion.

Simple meaning: O descendant of Bharata! When Lord Janardana appeared as the son of the couple, amongst the cowherd and gopis, the couple was intensely devoted to the Lord.

कृष्णो ब्रह्मण आदेशं सत्यं कर्तुं व्रजे विभुः ।

सहरामो वसंश्चक्रे तेषां प्रीतिं स्वलीलया ॥10.8.52॥

Word-by-word meaning: कृष्णः Krishna, ब्रह्मणः of Brahma, आदेशम् command, सत्यम् true, कर्तुम् to make, व्रजे in Vraja, विभुः the almighty, सह-रामः together with Rama, वसन् dwelling, चक्रे did, तेषाम् their, प्रीतिम् pleasure, स्व-लीलया by His own pastimes

Anvaya: विभुः कृष्णः ब्रह्मणः आदेशं सत्यं कर्तुं व्रजे सहरामः वसन् स्वलीलया तेषां प्रीतिं चक्रे।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: विभुः कृष्णः The almighty Krishna, ब्रह्मणः आदेशं सत्यं कर्तुम् to make true the command of Brahma, व्रजे in Vraja, रामः सह together with Rama, वसन् dwelling, स्वलीलया by His own pastimes, तेषां प्रीतिं चक्रे gave them pleasure.

Simple meaning: The almighty Krishna, to fulfill the command of Lord Brahma, dwelling in Vraja together with Rama, gave great pleasure to all of them through His own divine pastimes.

इति श्रीमद्भागवते महापुराणे पारमहंस्यां संहितायां दशमे स्कन्धे पूर्वार्धे

विश्वरूपदर्शनः नाम​ अष्टमः अध्यायः ॥8॥

Thus ends the eighth discourse, titled Vision of the Cosmic form (of the Lord), in the first half of Book Ten of the great and glorious Bhagavata-Purana.
