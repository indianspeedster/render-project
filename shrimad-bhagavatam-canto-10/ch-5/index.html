Canto 10

Chapter 5

The meeting of Nanda and Vasudeva

श्रीमद्भागवतमहापुराणम्

दशमः स्कन्धः

नन्दवसुदेवसंगमः नाम पञ्चमः अध्यायः

(32 Shlokas)

श्री शुक उवाच

नन्दस्त्वात्मज उत्पन्ने जाताह्लादो महामनाः ।

आहूय विप्रान् वेदज्ञान् स्नातः शुचिरलङ्कृतः ॥10.5.1॥

Word-by-word meaning: श्री शुकः उवाच Sri Shuka said, नन्दः Nanda, तु indeed, आत्मजे when son, उत्पन्ने was born, जात-आह्लादः filled with joy, महामनाः great minded, आहूय having called, विप्रान् brahmins, वेद-ज्ञान् knowers of the Vedas, स्नातः having bathed, शुचिः purified, अलङ्कृतः adorned

Anvaya: श्री शुकः उवाच — आत्मजे उत्पन्ने महामनाः नन्दः तु जाताह्लादः स्नातः शुचिः अलङ्कृतः वेदज्ञान् विप्रान् आहूय।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: श्री शुकः उवाच Sri Shuka said — आत्मजे उत्पन्ने When his son was born, महामनाः नन्दः तु the great minded Nanda indeed, जाताह्लादः filled with joy, स्नातः शुचिः अलङ्कृतः having bathed, purified and adorned himself, वेदज्ञान् विप्रान् आहूय summoned the brahmins who were knowers of the Vedas.

Simple meaning: Sri Shuka said: When his son was born, the great minded Nanda was filled with joy. Having bathed, purified and adorned himself, he summoned the brahmins who were learned in the Vedas.

वाचयित्वा स्वस्त्ययनं जातकर्मात्मजस्य वै ।

कारयामास विधिवत् पितृदेवार्चनं तथा ॥10.5.2॥

Word-by-word meaning: वाचयित्वा having caused to be recited, स्वस्ति-अयनम् benedictory hymns, जात-कर्म birth ceremony, आत्मजस्य of his son, वै indeed, कारयामास caused to be performed, विधिवत् according to prescribed rites, पितृ देव अर्चनम् worship of ancestors and gods, तथा also

Anvaya: स्वस्त्ययनं वाचयित्वा, आत्मजस्य वै जातकर्म, तथा पितृदेवार्चनं विधिवत् कारयामास।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: स्वस्त्ययनं वाचयित्वा Having caused benedictory hymns to be recited, आत्मजस्य वै जातकर्म his son's birth ceremony indeed, तथा पितृदेवार्चनं and also the worship of ancestors and gods, विधिवत् कारयामास he had them performed according to prescribed rites.

Simple meaning: Having had benedictory hymns recited, he had his son's birth ceremony performed according to the prescribed Vedic rites, along with the worship of ancestors and gods.

धेनूनां नियुते प्रादाद् विप्रेभ्य: समलङ्कृते ।

तिलाद्रीन् सप्त रत्नौघशातकौम्भाम्बरावृतान् ॥10.5.3॥

Word-by-word meaning: धेनूनाम् of cows, नियुते a hundred thousand, प्रादात् gave, विप्रेभ्यः to the brahmins, समलङ्कृते well adorned, तिल-अद्रीन् mountains of sesame, सप्त seven, रत्न-ओघ-शातकौम्भ-अम्बर-आवृतान् heaps of jewels, covered with golden cloth and garments

Anvaya: समलङ्कृते धेनूनां नियुते रत्नौघशातकौम्भाम्बरावृतान् सप्त तिलाद्रीन् विप्रेभ्यः प्रादात्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: समलङ्कृते धेनूनां नियुते A hundred thousand well adorned cows, रत्नौघशातकौम्भाम्बरावृतान् covered with heaps of jewels, golden cloth and garments, सप्त तिलाद्रीन् along with seven mountains of sesame, विप्रेभ्यः प्रादात् gave to the brahmins.

Simple meaning: He gave a hundred thousand well adorned cows to the brahmins, along with seven mountains of sesame seeds, heaps of jewels, covered with golden cloth and garments

कालेन स्नानशौचाभ्यां संस्कारैस्तपसेज्यया ।

शुध्यन्ति दानैः सन्तुष्ट्या द्रव्याण्यात्माऽऽत्मविद्यया ॥10.5.4॥

Word-by-word meaning: कालेन by time, स्नान-शौचाभ्याम् by bathing and cleanliness, संस्कारैः by sacraments, तपसा by austerity, इज्यया by worship, शुध्यन्ति are purified, दानैः by charity, सन्तुष्ट्या by contentment, द्रव्याणि material objects, आत्मा the soul, आत्म-विद्यया by self knowledge

Anvaya: द्रव्याणि कालेन स्नानशौचाभ्यां संस्कारैः तपसा इज्यया दानैः सन्तुष्ट्या शुध्यन्ति, आत्मा आत्मविद्यया।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: द्रव्याणि Material objects, कालेन स्नानशौचाभ्यां संस्कारैः तपसा इज्यया दानैः सन्तुष्ट्या शुध्यन्ति are purified by time, bathing and cleanliness, sacraments, austerity, worship, charity and contentment, आत्मा आत्मविद्यया the soul by self knowledge.

Simple meaning: Material objects are purified by time, bathing, cleanliness, sacraments, austerity, worship, charity and contentment, while the soul is purified by self-knowledge.

सौमङ्गल्यगिरो विप्राः सूतमागधवन्दिन ।

गायकाश्च जगुर्नेदुर्भेर्यो दुन्दुभयो मुहुः ॥10.5.5॥

Word-by-word meaning: सौमङ्गल्य-गिरः auspicious words, विप्राः brahmins, सूत-मागध-वन्दिनः bards, genealogists and panegyrists, गायकाः singers, च and, जगुः sang, नेदुः sounded, भेर्यः trumpets, दुन्दुभयः kettledrums, मुहुः repeatedly

१ - सूत पुराणोके कथा वाचक होते हैं।

२ - मागध वंशवली वर्णन करते हैं।

३ - वन्दी प्रसंगानुसार नवीन या पुरानी कविता पाठ करते हैं ।

Anvaya: विप्राः सौमङ्गल्यगिरः, सूतमागधवन्दिनः गायकाः च जगुः, भेर्यः दुन्दुभयः मुहुः नेदुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: विप्राः सौमङ्गल्यगिरः The brahmins chanted auspicious words, सूतमागधवन्दिनः गायकाः च जगुः the bards, genealogists, panegyrists and singers sang, भेर्यः दुन्दुभयः मुहुः नेदुः trumpets and kettledrums sounded repeatedly.

Simple meaning: The brahmins chanted auspicious benedictions, while the bards, genealogists, panegyrists and singers sang praises, and trumpets and kettledrums resounded again and again.

व्रजः सम्मृष्टसंसिक्तद्वाराजिरगृहान्तरः ।

चित्रध्वजपताकास्रक्चैलपल्लवतोरणैः ॥10.5.6॥

Word-by-word meaning: व्रजः Vraja, सम्मृष्ट-संसिक्त-द्वार-अजिर-गृह-अन्तरः well swept and sprinkled gateways, courtyards and interiors of the houses, चित्र-ध्वज-पताका-स्रक्-चैल-पल्लव-तोरणैः with various banners, garlands, cloths, leaves and festoons

Anvaya: व्रजः सम्मृष्टसंसिक्तद्वाराजिरगृहान्तरः चित्रध्वजपताकास्रक्चैलपल्लवतोरणैः …

Literal meaning of the anvaya: व्रजः Vraja, सम्मृष्टसंसिक्तद्वाराजिरगृहान्तरः with its gateways, courtyards, houses and interiors well swept and sprinkled, चित्रध्वजपताकास्रक्चैलपल्लवतोरणैः decorated with various flags, banners, garlands, cloths, leaves and festoons…

Simple meaning: Vraja, with its gateways, courtyards and houses thoroughly swept and sprinkled with water, was decorated with colorful flags, banners, garlands, cloths, leaves and festive arches.

गावो वृषा वत्सतरा हरिद्रातैलरूषिताः ।

विचित्र धातुबर्हस्रग्वस्त्रकाञ्चनमालिनः ॥10.5.7॥

Word-by-word meaning: गावः cows, वृषाः bulls, वत्सतराः calves, हरिद्रा-तैल-रूषिताः smeared with turmeric and oil, विचित्र-धातु-बर्ह-स्रक्-वस्त्र-काञ्चन-मालिनः adorned with various minerals, peacock feathers, garlands, cloths and golden necklaces

Anvaya: गावः वृषाः वत्सतराः हरिद्रातैलरूषिताः विचित्रधातुबर्हस्रग्वस्त्रकाञ्चनमालिनः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: गावः वृषाः वत्सतराः Cows, bulls and calves, हरिद्रातैलरूषिताः smeared with turmeric and oil, विचित्रधातुबर्हस्रग्वस्त्रकाञ्चनमालिनः adorned with various minerals, peacock feathers, garlands, cloths and golden necklaces.

Simple meaning: The cows, bulls and calves were smeared with turmeric and oil, and decorated with various colored minerals, peacock feathers, garlands, cloths and golden necklaces.

महार्हवस्त्राभरणकञ्चुकोष्णीषभूषिताः ।

गोपाः समाययू राजन् नानोपायनपाणयः ॥10.5.8॥

Word-by-word meaning: महा-अर्ह-वस्त्र-आभरण-कञ्चुक-उष्णीष-भूषिताः adorned with very valuable garments, ornaments, jackets and turbans, गोपाः cowherds, समाययुः came, राजन् O king (Shri Shuka is addressing Parikshita), नाना-उपायन-पाणयः with various gifts in hands

Anvaya: राजन्! महार्हवस्त्राभरणकञ्चुकोष्णीषभूषिताः गोपाः नानोपायनपाणयः समाययुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: राजन् O king, महार्हवस्त्राभरणकञ्चुकोष्णीषभूषिताः  adorned with very valuable garments, ornaments, jackets and turbans, गोपाः the cowherds, नानोपायनपाणयः  with various gifts in their hands, समाययुः came.

Simple meaning: O king! (Shri Shuka is addressing Parikshita) The cowherds, adorned with very valuable garments, ornaments, jackets and turbans, came with various gifts in their hands.

गोप्यश्चाकर्ण्य मुदिता यशोदायाः सुतोद्भवम् ।

आत्मानं भूषयाञ्चक्रुर्वस्त्राकल्पाञ्जनादिभिः ॥10.5.9॥

Word-by-word meaning: गोप्यः gopis, च and, आकर्ण्य having heard, मुदिताः delighted, यशोदायाः of Yashoda, सुत-उद्भवम् birth of son, आत्मानम् themselves, भूषयाञ्चक्रुः adorned, वस्त्र-आकल्प-अञ्जन-आदिभिः with garments, ornaments, collyrium etc.

Anvaya: गोप्यः च यशोदायाः सुतोद्भवम् आकर्ण्य मुदिताः वस्त्राकल्पाञ्जनादिभिः आत्मानं भूषयाञ्चक्रुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: गोप्यः च And the gopis, यशोदायाः सुतोद्भवम् आकर्ण्य having heard of the birth of Yashoda's son, मुदिताः delighted, वस्त्राकल्पाञ्जनादिभिः with garments, ornaments, collyrium and such things, आत्मानं भूषयाञ्चक्रुः adorned themselves.

Simple meaning: And the gopis, having heard of the birth of Yashoda's son, were delighted and adorned themselves with garments, ornaments, collyrium and other decorations.

नवकुङ्कुमकिञ्जल्कमुखपङ्कजभूतयः ।

बलिभिस्त्वरितं जग्मुः पृथुश्रोण्यश्चलत्कुचाः ॥10.5.10॥

Word-by-word meaning: नव-कुङ्कुम-किञ्जल्क-मुख-पङ्कज-भूतयः with faces adorned with fresh kumkum like lotuses with fresh pollen, बलिभिः with gifts, त्वरितम् quickly, जग्मुः went, पृथु-श्रोण्यः with broad hips, चलत्-कुचाः with moving breasts (on accord of their walking quickly)

Anvaya: नवकुङ्कुमकिञ्जल्कमुखपङ्कजभूतयः पृथुश्रोण्यः चलत्कुचाः बलिभिः त्वरितं जग्मुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: नवकुङ्कुमकिञ्जल्कमुखपङ्कजभूतयः With their faces adorned with fresh kumkum like lotuses with fresh pollen, पृथुश्रोण्यः  चलत्कुचाः with broad hips and moving breasts, बलिभिः त्वरितं जग्मुः quickly went with gifts.

Simple meaning: With their faces adorned with fresh kumkum like lotuses with fresh pollen, and with broad hips and swaying breasts, they quickly went carrying gifts.

गोप्यः सुमृष्टमणिकुण्डलनिष्ककण्ठ्यश्चित्राम्बराः पथि शिखाच्युतमाल्यवर्षाः ।

नन्दालयं सवलया व्रजतीर्विरेजुर्व्यालोल कुण्डलपयोधरहारशोभाः ॥10.5.11॥

Word-by-word meaning: गोप्यः gopis, सु-मृष्ट-मणि-कुण्डल-निष्क-कण्ठ्यः with well polished gem earrings, golden ornaments and necklaces, चित्र-अम्बराः in colorful garments, पथि on the path, शिखा-च्युत-माल्य-वर्षाः showering flowers falling from hair, नन्द-आलयम् to Nanda's abode, स-वलयाः with bangles, व्रजतीः going, विरेजुः shone, व्यालोल-कुण्डल-पयोधर-हार-शोभाः with the beauty of swaying earrings breasts and necklaces

Anvaya: सुमृष्टमणिकुण्डलनिष्ककण्ठ्यः चित्राम्बराः पथि शिखाच्युतमाल्यवर्षाः सवलयाः व्यालोलकुण्डलपयोधरहारशोभाः गोप्यः नन्दालयं व्रजतीः विरेजुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: सुमृष्टमणिकुण्डलनिष्ककण्ठ्यः चित्राम्बराः Adorned with well polished gem earrings, golden ornaments and necklaces, in colorful garments, पथि शिखाच्युतमाल्यवर्षाः showering flowers falling from their hair along the path, सवलयाः with bangles, व्यालोलकुण्डलपयोधरहारशोभाः with the beauty of their swaying earrings, breasts and necklaces, गोप्यः the gopis, नन्दालयं व्रजतीः going to Nanda's abode, विरेजुः shone.

Simple meaning: The gopis, adorned with well polished gem earrings, golden ornaments and necklaces, dressed in colorful garments and wearing bangles, shone radiantly as they walked to Nanda's abode, showering flowers from their hair along the path, with their swaying earrings, breasts and necklaces.

ता आशिषः प्रयुञ्जानाश्चिरं पाहीति बालके ।

हरिद्राचूर्णतैलाद्भिः सिञ्चन्त्यो जनमुज्जगुः ॥10.5.12॥

Word-by-word meaning: ताः they, आशिषः blessings, प्रयुञ्जानाः offering, चिरम् long, पाहि protect, इति thus, बालके upon the child, हरिद्रा-चूर्ण-तैल-अद्भिः with turmeric powder oil and water, सिञ्चन्त्यः sprinkling, जनम् people, उज्जगुः sang loudly

Anvaya: ताः बालके – चिरं पाहि – इति आशिषः प्रयुञ्जानाः हरिद्राचूर्णतैलाद्भिः जनं सिञ्चन्त्यः उज्जगुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: ताः बालके – चिरं पाहि – इति आशिषः प्रयुञ्जानाः They offering blessings upon the child "may you live long and be protected", हरिद्राचूर्णतैलाद्भिः जनं सिञ्चन्त्यः sprinkling with turmeric powder, oil and water on people, उज्जगुः sang loudly.

Simple meaning: Offering blessings upon the child saying "May you live long and be protected", and sprinkling turmeric powder, oil and water on the people, they sang loudly in celebration.

अवाद्यन्त विचित्राणि वादित्राणि महोत्सवे ।

कृष्णे विश्वेश्वरेऽनन्ते नन्दस्य व्रजमागते ॥10.5.13॥

Word-by-word meaning: अवाद्यन्त were played, विचित्राणि various, वादित्राणि musical instruments, महोत्सवे in the great festival, कृष्णे when Krishna, विश्वेश्वरे the Lord of the universe, अनन्ते the infinite one, नन्दस्य of Nanda, व्रजम् to Vraja, आगते had arrived

Anvaya: विश्वेश्वरे अनन्ते कृष्णे नन्दस्य व्रजम् आगते महोत्सवे विचित्राणि वादित्राणि अवाद्यन्त।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: विश्वेश्वरे अनन्ते कृष्णे When the Lord of the universe, the infinite one, Krishna, नन्दस्य व्रजम् आगते had come to Nanda’s Vraja, महोत्सवे in the great festival, विचित्राणि वादित्राणि अवाद्यन्त various musical instruments were played.

Simple meaning: When Krishna, the infinite Lord of the universe, arrived in Nanda’s Vraja, a grand festival was celebrated and many kinds of musical instruments resounded joyfully.

गोपाः परस्परं हृष्टा दधिक्षीरघृताम्बुभिः ।

आसिञ्चन्तो विलिम्पन्तो नवनीतैश्च चिक्षिपुः ॥10.5.14॥

Word-by-word meaning: गोपाः cowherds, परस्परम् one another, हृष्टाः joyful, दधि-क्षीर-घृत-अम्बुभिः with curd, with milk, with ghee, with water, आसिञ्चन्तः sprinkling, विलिम्पन्तः smearing, नवनीतैः with fresh butter, च and, चिक्षिपुः threw

Anvaya: हृष्टाः गोपाः परस्परं दधिक्षीरघृताम्बुभिः आसिञ्चन्तः विलिम्पन्तः च नवनीतैः चिक्षिपुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: हृष्टाः गोपाः joyful cowherds, परस्परं one another, दधिक्षीरघृताम्बुभिः आसिञ्चन्तः sprinkling with curd milk ghee and water, विलिम्पन्तः smearing, नवनीतैः चिक्षिपुः and throwing fresh butter.

Simple meaning: The joyful cowherds playfully splashed one another with curd, milk, ghee, and water, smeared each other, and threw fresh butter in festive delight.

नन्दो महामनास्तेभ्यो वासोऽलङ्कारगोधनम् ।

सूतमागधवन्दिभ्यो येऽन्ये विद्योपजीविनः ॥10.5.15॥

Word-by-word meaning: नन्दः Nanda, महामनाः magnanimous, तेभ्यः to them, वासः garments, अलङ्कार-गोधनम् ornaments and cows, सूत-मागध-वन्दिभ्यः to the bards, reciters and eulogists, ये those who, अन्ये others, विद्या-उपजीविनः living by knowledge

Anvaya: महामनाः नन्दः सूतमागधवन्दिभ्यः ये अन्ये विद्योपजीविनः तेभ्यः वासः अलङ्कारगोधनम् …

Literal meaning of the anvaya: महामनाः नन्दः The magnanimous Nanda, सूतमागधवन्दिभ्यः to the bards reciters and eulogists, ये अन्ये विद्योपजीविनः तेभ्यः and to others who lived by knowledge, to them, वासः अलङ्कारगोधनम् garments ornaments and cows …

Simple meaning: Generous-hearted Nanda, to the bards, reciters, eulogists, and all others who earned their livelihood through learning, gave garments, ornaments, and cows in charity …

तैस्तै कामैरदीनात्मा यथोचितमपूजयत् ।

विष्णोराराधनार्थाय स्वपुत्रस्योदयाय च ॥10.5.16॥

Word-by-word meaning: तैः तैः each with, कामैः desirable objects, अ-दीनात्मा without miserly spirit, यथा उचितम् appropriately, अपूजयत् worshiped/honored, विष्णोः of Vishnu, आराधना-अर्थाय for the purpose of worship, स्व-पुत्रस्य of his own son, उदयाय for the prosperity, च and

Anvaya: अदीनात्मा तैः तैः कामैः यथोचितं विष्णोः आराधनार्थाय स्वपुत्रस्य उदयाय च अपूजयत्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: अदीनात्मा The generous one, तैः तैः कामैः with various desirable objects, यथोचितम् appropriately honored, विष्णोः आराधनार्थाय for the purpose of worship of Vishnu, स्वपुत्रस्य उदयाय च and for the prosperity of his own son, अपूजयत् honored everyone.

Simple meaning: Without any trace of stinginess, Nanda properly honored everyone with various gifts that they desired, doing so for the worship of Vishnu and for the auspicious rise and prosperity of his newborn son.

रोहिणी च महाभागा नन्दगोपाभिनन्दिता ।

व्यचरद् दिव्यवासः स्रक्कण्ठाभरणभूषिता ॥10.5.17॥

Word-by-word meaning: रोहिणी Rohini, च and, महाभागा greatly blessed, नन्द-गोप-अभिनन्दिता honored by Nanda and the cowherds, व्यचरत् moved about, दिव्य-वासः wearing divine garments, स्रक्-कण्ठ-आभरण-भूषिता adorned with garlands and necklace

Anvaya: नन्दगोपाभिनन्दिता महाभागा रोहिणी च दिव्यवासः स्रक्कण्ठाभरणभूषिता व्यचरत्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: नन्दगोपाभिनन्दिता Honored by Nanda and the cowherds, महाभागा रोहिणी the greatly blessed Rohini, दिव्यवासः स्रक्कण्ठाभरणभूषिता adorned with divine garments garlands and ornaments for the neck, व्यचरत् moved about.

Simple meaning: Honored by Nanda and the cowherds, the greatly blessed Rohini, moved about beautifully adorned with divine garments, garlands, and shining ornaments.

तत आरभ्य नन्दस्य व्रजः सर्वसमृद्धिमान् ।

हरेर्निवासात्मगुणै रमाक्रीडमभून्नृप ॥10.5.18॥

Word-by-word meaning: ततः from that time, आरभ्य beginning, नन्दस्य of Nanda, व्रजः Vraja, सर्व-समृद्धिमान् endowed with all prosperity, हरेः of Hari, निवास dwelling place, आत्म-गुणैः by His own qualities, रमा-क्रीडम् playground of Lakshmi, अभूत् became, नृप O king

Anvaya: नृप! ततः आरभ्य नन्दस्य व्रजः सर्वसमृद्धिमान् हरेः निवास आत्मगुणैः रमाक्रीडम् अभूत्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: नृप O king, ततः आरभ्य from that time onward, नन्दस्य व्रजः Nanda’s Vraja, सर्वसमृद्धिमान् endowed with all prosperity, हरेः निवास being the dwelling place of Hari, आत्मगुणैः by His own qualities, रमाक्रीडम् अभूत् became the playground of Lakshmi.

Simple meaning: O king! From that time onward, Nanda’s Vraja became supremely prosperous, for it was the abode of Lord Hari, and by His divine qualities it turned into the very playground of Goddess Lakshmi.

गोपान् गोकुलरक्षायां निरूप्य मथुरां गतः ।

नन्दः कंसस्य वार्षिक्यं करं दातुं कुरूद्वह ॥10.5.19॥

Word-by-word meaning: गोपान् the cowherds, गोकुल-रक्षायाम् for the protection of Gokula, निरूप्य appointing, मथुराम् to Mathura, गतः went, नन्दः Nanda, कंसस्य of Kamsa, वार्षिक्यम् annual, करम् tax tribute, दातुम् to give, कुरूद्वह O best of the Kurus (addressing Parikshita)

Anvaya: कुरूद्वह! नन्दः गोकुलरक्षायां गोपान् निरूप्य, कंसस्य वार्षिक्यं करं दातुं मथुरां गतः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: कुरूद्वह O best of the Kurus!, नन्दः Nanda, गोकुलरक्षायां गोपान् निरूप्य appointing the cowherds for the protection of Gokula, कंसस्य वार्षिक्यं करं दातुं to give Kamsa’s annual tax, मथुरां गतः went to Mathura.

Simple meaning: O best of the Kurus, after appointing the cowherds to guard Gokula, Nanda went to Mathura to pay the yearly tribute due to Kamsa.

वसुदेव उपश्रुत्य भ्रातरं नन्दमागतम् ।

ज्ञात्वा दत्तकरं राज्ञे ययौ तदवमोचनम् ॥10.5.20॥

Word-by-word meaning: वसुदेवः Vasudeva, उपश्रुत्य hearing, भ्रातरम् brother, नन्दम् Nanda, आगतम् arrived, ज्ञात्वा knowing, दत्तकरम् having paid the tax, राज्ञे to the king, ययौ went, तत् his, अवमोचनम् place of release/lodging

Anvaya: वसुदेवः भ्रातरं नन्दम् आगतम् उपश्रुत्य राज्ञे दत्तकरं ज्ञात्वा तत् अवमोचनं ययौ ।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: वसुदेवः Vasudeva, भ्रातरं नन्दम् आगतम् उपश्रुत्य hearing that brother Nanda had arrived, राज्ञे दत्तकरं ज्ञात्वा knowing that he had paid the tax to the king, तत् अवमोचनं ययौ went to his lodging place.

Simple meaning: Vasudeva, upon hearing that brother Nanda had come and had paid the royal tax, went to meet him at his place of stay.

तं दृष्ट्वा सहसोत्थाय देहः प्राणमिवागतम् ।

प्रीतः प्रियतमं दोर्भ्यां सस्वजे प्रेमविह्वलः ॥10.5.21॥

Word-by-word meaning: तम् him, दृष्ट्वा seeing, सहसा immediately, उत्थाय rising, देहः body, प्राणम् life air, इव like, आगतम् returned, प्रीतः delighted, प्रियतमम् most dear one, दोर्भ्याम् with both arms, सस्वजे embraced, प्रेम-विह्वलः overwhelmed with love

Anvaya: प्रियतमं तं दृष्ट्वा देहः प्राणम् आगतम् इव सहसा उत्थाय, प्रीतः प्रेमविह्वलः दोर्भ्यां सस्वजे।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: प्रियतमं तं दृष्ट्वा Seeing him (Vasudeva) who was most beloved, देहः प्राणम् आगतम् इव like a body in which life air has returned, सहसा उत्थाय rising immediately, प्रीतः delighted (Nanda), प्रेमविह्वलः overwhelmed with love, दोर्भ्यां सस्वजे embraced with both arms.

Simple meaning: On seeing Vasudeva, Nanda at once rose as if it were a lifeless body revived by the return of life. Filled with joy and overcome with love, Nanda warmly embraced his dearest friend Vasudeva.

पूजितः सुखमासीनः पृष्ट्वानामयमादृतः ।

प्रसक्तधीः स्वात्मजयोरिदमाह विशाम्पते ॥10.5.22॥

Word-by-word meaning: पूजितः honored, सुखम् comfortably, आसीनः seated, पृष्ट्वा after inquiring, अनामयम् about well-being, आदृतः respectfully, प्रसक्तधीः deeply attached in mind, स्व-आत्मजयोः to his own sons (Krishna and Balarama), इदम् this, आह said, विशाम्पते O lord of the people (Addressing Parikshita)

Anvaya: विशाम्पते! पूजितः सुखम् आसीनः आदृतः अनामयं पृष्ट्वा स्वात्मजयोः प्रसक्तधीः इदम् आह।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: विशाम्पते O lord of the people! पूजितः Honored, सुखम् आसीनः seated comfortably, आदृतः respectfully, अनामयं पृष्ट्वा after inquiring about well-being, स्वात्मजयोः प्रसक्तधीः being deeply attached to his own sons, इदम् आह said this.

Simple meaning: O lord of the people! After being properly honored and seated comfortably, and after respectfully inquiring about Nanda’s welfare, Vasudeva—whose mind was deeply attached to his sons (Krishna and Balarama) —spoke the following words.

दिष्ट्याभ्रातः प्रवयस इदानीमप्रजस्य ते ।

प्रजाशाया निवृत्तस्य प्रजा यत् समपद्यत ॥10.5.23॥

Word-by-word meaning: दिष्ट्या by good fortune, भ्रातः O brother, प्र-वयसः of advanced in age, इदानीम् now, अ-प्रजस्य without progeny, ते of you, प्रजा-आशायाः from the hope for a child, निवृत्तस्य who had given up, प्रजा offspring, यत् that, समपद्यत has been obtained

Anvaya: भ्रातः! प्रवयसः अप्रजस्य ते प्रजाशायाः निवृत्तस्य दिष्ट्या इदानीं यत् प्रजा समपद्यत।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: भ्रातः O brother! प्रवयसः अप्रजस्य ते Of you who are advanced in age and were without progeny, प्रजाशायाः निवृत्तस्य you who had given hope of obtaining a child, दिष्ट्या it is with great fortune, यत् इदानीं प्रजा समपद्यत that, now you have obtained a child.

Simple meaning: O brother! By great good fortune, even though you have grown old and had given up hope of having children, you have now been blessed with a child.

दिष्ट्या संसारचक्रेऽस्मिन् वर्तमानः पुनर्भवः ।

उपलब्धो भवानद्य दुर्लभं प्रियदर्शनम् ॥10.5.24॥

Word-by-word meaning: दिष्ट्या by good fortune, संसार-चक्रे in the cycle of worldly existence, अस्मिन् in this, वर्तमानः existing, पुनर्भवः rebirth, उपलब्धः obtained, भवान् you, अद्य today, दुर्लभम् rare, प्रिय-दर्शनम् the sight of a dear one

Anvaya: अस्मिन् संसारचक्रे दिष्ट्या भवान् अद्य उपलब्धः।  प्रियदर्शनं दुर्लभम् (अस्ति)। वर्तमानः पुनर्भवः (अस्ति)।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: अस्मिन् संसारचक्रे In this life-time, दिष्ट्या by good fortune, भवान् अद्य उपलब्धः today, I have met you. प्रियदर्शनं दुर्लभम् (अस्ति) Sight of a dear one is rare. वर्तमानः पुनर्भवः (अस्ति) This is like a rebirth (to me).

Simple meaning: By good fortune, I have been able to meet you today. The sight of a dear one is rare. This is like a rebirth for me.

नैकत्र प्रियसंवासः सुहृदां चित्रकर्मणाम् ।

ओघेन व्यूह्यमानानां प्लवानां स्रोतसो यथा ॥10.5.25॥

Word-by-word meaning: न not, एकत्र in one place, प्रिय-संवासः living together happily, सुहृदाम् of friends, चित्र-कर्मणाम् whose actions are varied (or whose destinies are diverse), ओघेन by a flood/current, व्यूह्यमानानाम् being swept away, प्लवानाम् of floating objects (like logs), स्रोतसः in a stream, यथा just as

Anvaya: यथा स्रोतसः ओघेन व्यूह्यमानानां प्लवानाम् एकत्र प्रियसंवासः न (भवति), तथा चित्रकर्मणां सुहृदाम् (एकत्र प्रियसंवासः न भवति)।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: यथा स्रोतसः Just as, in a river, ओघेन व्यूह्यमानानां being carried away by the current, प्लवानाम् floating objects, एकत्र प्रियसंवासः न (भवति) do not remain happily together in one place, तथा चित्रकर्मणां सुहृदाम् similarly friends, guided by their varied destinies, (एकत्र प्रियसंवासः न भवति) (do not remain together in one place).

Simple meaning: Just as pieces of wood floating in a river are carried apart by the current and cannot stay together, in the same way friends, guided by their varied destinies, cannot always live together in one place.

कच्चित् पशव्यं निरुजं भूर्यम्बुतृणवीरुधम् ।

बृहद्वनं तदधुना यत्रास्से त्वं सुहृद्वृतः ॥10.5.26॥

Word-by-word meaning: कच्चित् whether (I hope), पशव्यम् suitable for cattle, निरुजम् free from disease or trouble, भूरि abundant, अम्बु-तृण-वीरुधम् water, grass and vegetation, बृहत् वनम् great forest, तत् that, अधुना now, यत्र where, आस्से you are dwelling, त्वम् you, सुहृत्-वृतः surrounded by friends

आस्से - आस् , कर्तरि लट्लकारः (आत्मनेपदम्) , मध्यमपुरुषः , एकवचनम् src. Dhatupath, Ashtadhyayi

Anvaya: यत्र त्वं सुहृद्वृतः अधुना आस्से, तत् बृहद्वनम् पशव्यं निरुजं भूर्यम्बुतृणवीरुधम् (अस्ति) कच्चित्?

Literal meaning of the anvaya: यत्र त्वं सुहृद्वृतः अधुना आस्से Where are you are living now, surrounded by you friends, तत् बृहद्वनम् that great forest, पशव्यं निरुजं भूर्यम्बुतृणवीरुधम् (अस्ति) कच्चित् is suitable for cattle, free from disease, and abundant in water, grass, and vegetation, I hope.

Simple meaning: I hope that the great forest where you now live with your friends is rich in water, grass, and plants, free from diseases and well suited for your cattle.

भ्रातर्मम सुतः कच्चिन्मात्रा सह भवद्व्रजे ।

तातं भवन्तं मन्वानो भवद्भ्यामुपलालितः ॥10.5.27॥

Word-by-word meaning: भ्रातः O brother, मम my, सुतः son, कच्चित् whether (I hope), मात्रा with his mother, सह together, भवत्-व्रजे in your Vraja, तातम् father, भवन्तम् you, मन्वानः considering, भवद्भ्याम् by both of you, उपलालितः being cared for (fondled, cherished)

Anvaya: भ्रातः! मम सुतः मात्रा सह भवद्व्रजे भवद्भ्याम् उपलालितः भवन्तं तातं मन्वानः कच्चित्?

Literal meaning of the anvaya: भ्रातः O brother! मम सुतः My son, मात्रा सह together with his mother, भवद्व्रजे in your Vraja, भवद्भ्याम् उपलालितः being affectionately cared for by both of you, भवन्तं तातं मन्वानः कच्चित् considering you as his father, hope he is alright.

Simple meaning: O brother! I hope that my son along with his mother, in your Vraja, being lovingly raised by both of you, considering you as his father, is alright.

पुंसस्त्रिवर्गो विहितः सुहृदो ह्यनुभावितः ।

न तेषु क्लिश्यमानेषु त्रिवर्गोऽर्थाय कल्पते ॥10.5.28॥

Word-by-word meaning: पुंसः for a man, त्रिवर्गः the three goals of life (dharma, artha, kama), विहितः prescribed, सुहृदः of friends/relatives, हि indeed, अनुभावितः experienced or made meaningful, न not, तेषु when they, क्लिश्यमानेषु are suffering, त्रिवर्गः the three goals of life, अर्थाय for any benefit, कल्पते becomes fit or useful

Anvaya: हि पुंसः सुहृदः अनुभावितः त्रिवर्गः विहितः, तेषु क्लिश्यमानेषु त्रिवर्गः अर्थाय न कल्पते।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: हि पुंसः For a man, सुहृदः अनुभावितः experienced through (or made meaningful by) friends and relatives, त्रिवर्गः विहितः the three aims of life are prescribed. तेषु क्लिश्यमानेषु When they are suffering, त्रिवर्गः अर्थाय न कल्पते the three aims of life do not become useful for any purpose.

Simple meaning: The three goals of life for a man—dharma, wealth, and pleasure—are meant to be enjoyed in the company of loved ones. If one’s friends and relatives are in distress, these goals bring no real benefit.

नन्द उवाच

अहो ते देवकीपुत्राः कंसेन बहवो हताः ।

एकावशिष्टावरजा कन्या सापि दिवं गता ॥10.5.29॥

Word-by-word meaning: नन्दः उवाच Nanda said, अहो alas, ते your, देवकी-पुत्राः the sons of Devaki, कंसेन by Kamsa, बहवः many, हताः were killed, एका one, अवशिष्टा remained, अवरजा the younger (child), कन्या daughter, सा she, अपि also, दिवम् to heaven, गता went

Anvaya: नन्दः उवाच – अहो! ते देवकीपुत्राः बहवः कंसेन हताः। एका अवरजा कन्या अवशिष्टा, सा अपि दिवं गता।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: नन्दः उवाच Nanda said: अहो! Alas! ते देवकीपुत्राः बहवः कंसेन हताः Many of your and Devaki’s sons were killed by Kamsa. एका अवरजा कन्या अवशिष्टा One younger daughter remained, सा अपि दिवं गता she also went to heaven.

Simple meaning: Nanda said: Alas, many of your and Devaki’s sons were slain by Kamsa. Only one younger daughter survived, and she too went to heaven.

नूनं ह्यदृष्टनिष्ठोऽयमदृष्टपरमो जनः।

अदृष्टमात्मनस्तत्त्वं यो वेद न स मुह्यति ॥10.5.30॥

Word-by-word meaning: नूनम् indeed, हि certainly, अदृष्ट-निष्ठः one who dependent on destiny (the unseen), अयम् a person, अदृष्ट-परमः one whose destiny is ultimate, जनः a person, अदृष्टम् destiny (the unseen result of past actions), आत्मनः of oneself, तत्त्वम् the true nature or reality, यः who, वेद knows, न not, सः he, मुह्यति becomes bewildered

Anvaya: नूनं हि अयं जनः अदृष्टनिष्ठः अदृष्टपरमः (भवति)। यः आत्मनः तत्त्वं अदृष्टं (इति) वेद, सः न मुह्यति।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: नूनं हि Indeed, अयं जनः a person, अदृष्टनिष्ठः is dependent on destiny, अदृष्टपरमः (भवति) and his destiny is the ultimate. यः He who, आत्मनः तत्त्वं his own truth, अदृष्टं (इति) वेद knows to be as his destiny, सः न मुह्यति does not become bewildered.

Simple meaning: Indeed, a person is dependent on his destiny and is controlled by it. One who realises that this his truth, he is not under any delusion.

वसुदेव उवाच

करो वै वार्षिको दत्तो राज्ञे दृष्टा वयं च वः ।

नेह स्थेयं बहुतिथं सन्त्युत्पाताश्च गोकुले ॥10.5.31॥

Word-by-word meaning: वसुदेवः उवाच Vasudeva said, करः tax, वै indeed, वार्षिकः annual, दत्तः given, राज्ञे to the king, दृष्टाः seen, वयम् we, च and, वः you, न not, इह here, स्थेयम् should remain, बहु-तिथम् for many days, सन्ति there are, उत्पाताः disturbances, च and, गोकुले in Gokula

Anvaya: वसुदेवः उवाच – वै राज्ञे वार्षिकः करः दत्तः, वयं च वः दृष्टाः। इह बहुतिथं न स्थेयम्। गोकुले च उत्पाताः सन्ति।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: वसुदेवः उवाच Vasudeva said: वै राज्ञे वार्षिकः करः दत्तः Indeed, the annual tax has been given to the king, वयं च वः दृष्टाः and we have seen you. इह बहुतिथं न स्थेयम् You should not remain here for many days. गोकुले च उत्पाताः सन्ति There are disturbances in Gokula.

Simple meaning: Vasudeva said: The yearly tax has been paid to the king, and we have met you. We should not stay here long, for there are troubling signs in Gokula.

श्री शुक उवाच

इति नन्दादयो गोपाः प्रोक्तास्ते शौरिणा ययुः ।

अनोभिरनडुद्युक्तैस्तमनुज्ञाप्य गोकुलम् ॥10.5.32॥

Word-by-word meaning: श्री शुक उवाच Sri Shuka said, इति thus, नन्द-आदयः Nanda and others, गोपाः the cowherds, प्रोक्ताः having been addressed, ते they, शौरिणा by Shouri (Vasudeva), ययुः went, अनोभिः by carts, अनडुह्-युक्तैः yoked with oxen, तम् him, अनुज्ञाप्य taking permission from him, गोकुलम् to Gokula

अनोभिः - शकटम् - अनस्,  सकारान्तः, नपुंसकलिङ्गः, असन्तः, तृतीयाबहुवचनम् src. Shabdapath. Ashtadhyayi

अनडुह् हकारान्तः, पुंलिङ्गः - वृषभः src. Shabdapath. Ashtadhyayi

अनडुद्युक्तैः जश्त्वसन्धिः

Anvaya: श्री शुक उवाच – इति शौरिणा प्रोक्ताः नन्दादयः गोपाः तम् अनुज्ञाप्य अनडुद्युक्तैः अनोभिः गोकुलं ययुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: श्री शुक उवाच Sri Shuka said: इति शौरिणा प्रोक्ताः Thus addressed by Shouri, नन्दादयः गोपाः Nanda and the other cowherds, तम् अनुज्ञाप्य having taken his permission,  अनडुद्युक्तैः अनोभिः in carts yoked with oxen, गोकुलं ययुः went to Gokula.

Simple meaning: Sri Shuka said: After being instructed by Shouri, Nanda and the other cowherds took his leave and returned to Gokula in their ox-drawn carts.

श्रीमद्भागवते महापुराणे पारमहंस्यां संहितायां दशमे स्कन्धे पूर्वार्धे

नन्दवसुदेवसंगमः नाम पञ्चमः अध्यायः ॥5॥

Thus ends the fifth discourse entitled 'The meeting of Nanda and Vasudeva', in the first half of Book Ten of the great and glorious Bhagavata-Purana.
