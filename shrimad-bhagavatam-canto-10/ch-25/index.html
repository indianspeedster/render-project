अध्यायः २५

ॐ

॥ॐ॥

वसुदेवसुतं देवं कंसचाणूरमर्दनम् ।

देवकी परमानन्दं कृष्णं वन्दे जगद्गुरुम् ॥

Shrimad Bhagwatam - Book 1

Canto 10

Chapter 25

The Lord lifts up Mount Govardhana

श्रीमद्भागवतमहापुराणम्

दशमः स्कन्धः

गोवर्धनधारणलीला नाम पञ्चविंशः अध्यायः

(33 Shlokas)

श्री शुक उवाच

इन्द्रस्तदाऽऽत्मनः पूजां विज्ञाय विहतां नृप ।

गोपेभ्यः कृष्णनाथेभ्यो नन्दादिभ्यश्चुकोप सः ॥10.25.1॥

Word-by-word meaning: श्री शुक उवाच Shri Shuka said, इन्द्रः Indra, तदा then, आत्मनः his own, पूजाम् worship, विज्ञाय understanding, विहताम् frustrated, नृप O King (Parikshit), गोपेभ्यः at the cowherd men, कृष्ण-नाथेभ्यः who had Krishna as their protector, नन्द-आदिभ्यः headed by Nanda Maharaja, चुकोप became angry, सः he

Anvaya: नृप! तदा सः इन्द्रः आत्मनः पूजां विहतां विज्ञाय कृष्णनाथेभ्यः नन्दादिभ्यः गोपेभ्यः चुकोप।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: नृप O King, तदा then, सः इन्द्रः that Indra, आत्मनः पूजां his own worship, विहतां frustrated, विज्ञाय understanding, कृष्णनाथेभ्यः having Krishna as their master, नन्दादिभ्यः headed by Nanda, गोपेभ्यः at the cowherds, चुकोप became angry.

Simple meaning: Shri Shuka said: O King Parikshit, when Indra realized that his worship had been stopped, he became terribly angry with Nanda and the other cowherd men, who had Krishna as their protector.

गणं सांवर्तकं नाम मेघानां चान्तकारिणाम् ।

इन्द्रः प्राचोदयत् क्रुद्धो वाक्यं चाहेशमान्युत ॥10.25.2॥

Word-by-word meaning: गणम् the group, सांवर्तकम् Samvartaka, नाम named, मेघानाम् of clouds, च and, अन्त-कारिणाम् which cause destruction (at the end of the universe), इन्द्रः Indra, प्राचोदयत् sent forth, क्रुद्धः angry, वाक्यम् words, च and, आह spoke, ईश-मानी thinking himself the supreme controller, उत indeed

Anvaya: क्रुद्धः ईशमानी उत इन्द्रः अन्तकारिणां मेघानां सांवर्तकं नाम गणं प्राचोदयत्। वाक्यं च आह।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: क्रुद्धः Angry, ईशमानी thinking himself the supreme lord, उत indeed, इन्द्रः Indra, अन्तकारिणां मेघानां of the clouds of destruction, सांवर्तकं नाम known as Samvartaka, गणं the group, प्राचोदयत् sent forth. वाक्यं च आह and spoke (these) words.

Simple meaning: Being falsely proud of his own power and becoming extremely angry, Indra sent forth the group of devastating clouds known as Samvartaka, which are usually reserved for the time of universal destruction. He then spoke to them as follows.

अहो श्रीमदमाहात्म्यं गोपानां काननौकसाम् ।

कृष्णं मर्त्यमुपाश्रित्य ये चक्रुर्देवहेलनम् ॥10.25.3॥

Word-by-word meaning: अहो Oh, श्री-मद-माहात्म्यम् the great intoxication caused by material opulence, गोपानाम् of the cowherd men, कानन-ओकसाम् who are dwellers of the forest, कृष्णम् Krishna, मर्त्यम् a mere mortal, उपाश्रित्य taking shelter of, ये who, चक्रुः have committed, देव-हेलनम् insult to the gods

Anvaya: अहो! काननौकसां गोपानां श्रीमदमाहात्म्यं ये मर्त्यं कृष्णम् उपाश्रित्य देवहेलनं चक्रुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: अहो Oh, काननौकसां गोपानां of the forest-dwellers cowherds, श्रीमदमाहात्म्यं the great pride due to wealth, ये who, मर्त्यं कृष्णम् उपाश्रित्य taking shelter of a mortal, Krishna, देवहेलनं चक्रुः have done insult to the gods.

Simple meaning: Indra said: Oh, just look at the enormous pride of these cowherds living in the forest! Taking shelter of a mere mortal like Krishna, they have dared to insult the gods.

यथादृढैः कर्ममयैः क्रतुभिर्नामनौनिभैः।

विद्यामान्वीक्षिकीं हित्वा तितीर्षन्ति भवार्णवम् ॥10.25.4॥

Word-by-word meaning: यथा just as, अदृढैः fragile, कर्म-मयैः consisting of fruitive activities, क्रतुभिः by ritual sacrifices, नाम-नौ-निभैः which are like boats in name only, विद्याम् knowledge, आन्वीक्षिकीम् spiritual (of the self), हित्वा abandoning, तितीर्षन्ति they desire to cross, भव-अर्णवम् the ocean of material existence

Anvaya: यथा आन्वीक्षिकीं विद्यां हित्वा अदृढैः कर्ममयैः नामनौनिभैः क्रतुभिः भवार्णवं तितीर्षन्ति।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: यथा Just as, आन्वीक्षिकीं विद्यां spiritual knowledge of the self, हित्वा abandoning, अदृढैः fragile, कर्ममयैः based on fruitive work, नामनौनिभैः like boats in name only, क्रतुभिः by ritual sacrifices, भवार्णवं the ocean of material existence, तितीर्षन्ति they desire to cross.

Simple meaning: Indra compared the cowherds' actions to foolish people who abandon spiritual knowledge and instead try to cross the vast ocean of material existence using fragile ritual sacrifices, which are like boats that exist in name only but cannot actually float.

वाचालं बालिशं स्तब्धमज्ञं पण्डितमानिनम् ।

कृष्णं मर्त्यमुपाश्रित्य गोपा मे चक्रुरप्रियम् ॥10.25.5॥

Word-by-word meaning: वाचालम् talkative, बालिशम् childish, स्तब्धम् arrogant, अज्ञम् ignorant, पण्डित-मानिनम् thinking Himself to be a learned scholar, कृष्णम् Krishna, मर्त्यम् a mortal, उपाश्रित्य taking shelter of, गोपाः the cowherds, मे to me, चक्रुः have done, अप्रियम् offense (displeasure)

Anvaya: गोपाः वाचालं बालिशं स्तब्धम् अज्ञं पण्डितमानिनं मर्त्यं कृष्णम् उपाश्रित्य मे अप्रियंं चक्रुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: गोपाः The cowherds, वाचालं बालिशं talkative, childish, स्तब्धम् अज्ञं arrogant, ignorant, पण्डितमानिनं thinking Himself a wise scholar, मर्त्यं कृष्णम् mortal Krishna, उपाश्रित्य taking shelter of, मे to me, अप्रियंं चक्रुः have done offense.

Simple meaning: Indra said: These cowherds have acted for my displeasure by taking shelter of this talkative, childish, arrogant, and ignorant Krishna, who is a mere mortal yet thinks Himself to be a great scholar.

एषां श्रियावलिप्तानां कृष्णेनाध्यायितात्मनाम् ।

धुनुत श्रीमदस्तम्भं पशून् नयत संक्षयम् ॥10.25.6॥

Word-by-word meaning: एषाम् of these (cowherds), श्रिया-अवलिप्तानाम् puffed up by material opulence, कृष्णेन by Krishna, अध्यायित-आत्मनाम् whose minds are encouraged, धुनुत destroy, श्री-मद-स्तम्भम् the arrogance caused by wealth, पशून् their animals, नयत lead, संक्षयम् to destruction

Anvaya: कृष्णेन अध्यायितात्मनां श्रियावलिप्तानाम् एषां श्रीमदस्तम्भं धुनुत। पशून् संक्षयं नयत।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: कृष्णेन By Krishna, अध्यायितात्मनां whose minds are encouraged, श्रियावलिप्तानाम् puffed up by wealth, एषाम् of these (cowherds), श्रीमदस्तम्भं धुनुत destroy the arrogance of material opulence. पशून् संक्षयं नयत Lead their animals to destruction.

Simple meaning: Indra commanded the clouds: These cowherds have become arrogant due to their wealth and are being encouraged by Krishna. Go and destroy their foolish pride and bring their animals to destruction.

अहं चैरावतं नागमारुह्यानुव्रजे व्रजम् ।

मरुद्गणैर्महावीर्यैर्नन्दगोष्ठजिघांसया ॥10.25.7॥

Word-by-word meaning: अहम् I, च and, ऐरावतम् Airavata, नागम् the elephant, आरुह्य mounting, अनुव्रजे shall follow, व्रजम् to Vraja, मरुद्गणैः with the groups of Wind-gods, महा-वीर्यैः who are very powerful, नन्द-गोष्ठ-जिघांसया with the intent to destroy the cowherd community of Nanda

Anvaya: अहं च महावीर्यैः मरुद्गणैः सह नन्दगोष्ठजिघांसया ऐरावतं नागम् आरुह्य व्रजम् अनुव्रजे।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: अहं च And I, महावीर्यैः मरुद्गणैः with very powerful groups of Maruts (Wind-gods), नन्दगोष्ठजिघांसया with the desire to destroy Nanda's cowherd settlement, ऐरावतं नागम् आरुह्य mounting the elephant Airavata, व्रजम् अनुव्रजे shall follow to Vraja.

Simple meaning: Indra said: I also shall follow you to Vraja, riding on my elephant Airavata and accompanied by the powerful Maruts. I will go there with the full intent to destroy the cowherd community of Nanda.

श्री शुक उवाच

इत्थं मघवताऽऽज्ञप्ता मेघा निर्मुक्तबन्धनाः ।

नन्दगोकुलमासारैः पीडयामासुरोजसा ॥10.25.8॥

Word-by-word meaning: श्री शुक उवाच Shri Shuka said, इत्थम् in this manner, मघवता by Indra, आज्ञप्ताः commanded, मेघाः the clouds, निर्मुक्त-बन्धनाः released from their bonds, नन्द-गोकुलम् the cowherd village of Nanda, आसारैः with downpours of rain, पीडयामासुः tormented, ओजसा with great force

Anvaya: इत्थं मघवता आज्ञप्ताः निर्मुक्तबन्धनाः मेघाः ओजसा आसारैः नन्दगोकुलं पीडयामासुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: इत्थं In this way, मघवता by Indra, आज्ञप्ताः commanded, निर्मुक्तबन्धनाः released from their restraints, मेघाः the clouds, ओजसा with great power, आसारैः with driving rains, नन्दगोकुलं पीडयामासुः afflicted the cowherd settlement of Nanda.

Simple meaning: Shri Shuka said: Being thus commanded by Indra, the clouds were released from their bonds and began to torment Nanda's village of Gokula with great force, using powerful downpours of rain.

विद्योतमाना विद्युद्भिः स्तनन्तः स्तनयित्नुभिः ।

तीव्रैर्मरुद्गणैर्नुन्ना ववृषुर्जलशर्कराः ॥10.25.9॥

Word-by-word meaning: विद्योतमानाः flashing, विद्युद्भिः with lightning, स्तनन्तः roaring, स्तनयित्नुभिः with thunder, तीव्रैः by the fierce, मरुद्गणैः by groups of winds, नुन्नाः driven, ववृषुः they rained down, जल-शर्कराः hailstones (literally, "water-pebbles")

Anvaya: विद्युद्भिः विद्योतमानाः स्तनयित्नुभिः स्तनन्तः तीव्रैः मरुद्गणैः नुन्नाः जलशर्कराः ववृषुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: विद्युद्भिः विद्योतमानाः With lightning, flashing, स्तनयित्नुभिः स्तनन्तः with thunder, roaring, तीव्रैः मरुद्गणैः by fierce groups of winds, नुन्नाः driven, जलशर्कराः hailstones, ववृषुः they rained down.

Simple meaning: Flashing with lightning and roaring with thunder, the clouds were driven by fierce winds and rained down sharp hailstones.

स्थूणास्थूला वर्षधारा मुञ्चत्स्वभ्रेष्वभीक्ष्णशः ।

जलौघैः प्लाव्यमाना भूर्नादृश्यत नतोन्नतम् ॥10.25.10॥

Word-by-word meaning: स्थूणा-स्थूलाः as thick as pillars, वर्ष-धाराः torrents of rain, मुञ्चत्सु emitting, अभ्रेषु the clouds, अभीक्ष्णशः incessantly, जल-ओघैः by the floods of water, प्लाव्यमाना inundated, भूः the earth, न not, अदृश्यत could be seen, नत-उन्नतम् the high and low points (valleys and hills)

Anvaya: अभ्रेषु अभीक्ष्णशः स्थूणास्थूलाः वर्षधाराः मुञ्चत्सु जलौघैः प्लाव्यमाना भूः नतोन्नतं न अदृश्यत।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: अभ्रेषु The clouds, अभीक्ष्णशः incessantly, स्थूणास्थूलाः वर्षधाराः मुञ्चत्सु thick as pillars, torrents of rain, emitting, जलौघैः by the floods of water, प्लाव्यमाना being inundated, भूः the earth, नतोन्नतं the low and high places, न अदृश्यत could not be seen.

Simple meaning: As the clouds incessantly poured down torrents of rain as thick as pillars, the entire earth became so inundated with floods of water that one could no longer distinguish the high ground from the low.

अत्यासारातिवातेन पशवो जातवेपनाः ।

गोपा गोप्यश्च शीतार्ता गोविन्दं शरणं ययुः ॥10.25.11॥

Word-by-word meaning: अति-आसार-अति-वातेन by the excessive rain and excessive wind, पशवः the animals, जात-वेपनाः began to tremble, गोपाः the cowherd men, गोप्यः the cowherd women, च and, शीत-आर्ताः suffering from the cold, गोविन्दम् Govinda, शरणम् for shelter, ययुः went

Anvaya: अत्यासारातिवातेन पशवः जातवेपनाः शीतार्ताः गोपाः गोप्यः च गोविन्दं शरणं ययुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: अत्यासारातिवातेन By the extreme downpour and extreme wind, पशवः the animals, जातवेपनाः started trembling, शीतार्ताः suffering from the cold, गोपाः the cowherds, गोप्यः the cowherd women, च and, गोविन्दं शरणं to Govinda, for shelter, ययुः went.

Simple meaning: Due to the excessive rain and fierce winds, the animals began to tremble. Suffering from the intense cold, the cowherd men and women all went to Govinda to take shelter.

शिरः सुतांश्च कायेन प्रच्छाद्यासारपीडिताः ।

वेपमाना भगवतः पादमूलमुपाययुः ॥10.25.12॥

Word-by-word meaning: शिरः their heads, सुतान् their children, च and, कायेन with their bodies, प्रच्छाद्य covering, आसार-पीडिताः distressed by the heavy rain, वेपमानाः trembling, भगवतः of the Supreme Lord, पाद-मूलम् to the soles of the feet, उपाययुः approached

Anvaya: आसारपीडिताः शिरः कायेन सुतान् च प्रच्छाद्य वेपमानाः भगवतः पादमूलम् उपाययुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: आसारपीडिताः Distressed by the downpour, शिरः their heads, कायेन with their bodies, सुतान् children, च and, प्रच्छाद्य covering, वेपमानाः trembling, भगवतः of the Supreme Lord, पादमूलम् the base of the feet, उपाययुः approached.

Simple meaning: Distressed by the heavy downpour, the cowherds tried to protect their own heads and their children by covering them with their bodies. Trembling from the cold, they all approached the lotus feet of the Supreme Lord Krishna for protection.

कृष्ण कृष्ण महाभाग त्वन्नाथं गोकुलं प्रभो ।

त्रातुमर्हसि देवान्नः कुपिताद् भक्तवत्सल ॥10.25.13॥

Word-by-word meaning: कृष्ण Krishna, कृष्ण Krishna, महा-भाग O most fortunate one, त्वत्-नाथम् having You as its master, गोकुलम् the community of Gokula, प्रभो O Lord, त्रातुम् to protect, अर्हसि You should, देवात् from the demigod (Indra), नः us, कुपितात् who is angry, भक्त-वत्सल O You who are very affectionate to Your devotees.

Anvaya: कृष्ण! कृष्ण! महाभाग! प्रभो! भक्तवत्सल! त्वन्नाथं गोकुलं नः कुपितात् देवात् त्रातुम् अर्हसि।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: कृष्ण कृष्ण Krishna, Krishna, महाभाग O most fortunate one, प्रभो O Lord, भक्तवत्सल O lover of Your devotees, त्वन्नाथं गोकुलं having You as its protector Gokula, नः us, कुपितात् देवात् from the angry god, त्रातुम् अर्हसि ought to protect.

Simple meaning: The cowherds prayed: O Krishna, Krishna! O most fortunate one! O Lord, You are so affectionate to Your devotees. Please protect us and the community of Gokula, which has You as its master, from the wrath of the angry demigod Indra.

शिलावर्षनिपातेन हन्यमानमचेतनम् ।

निरीक्ष्य भगवान् मेने कुपितेन्द्रकृतं हरिः ॥10.25.14॥

Word-by-word meaning: शिला-वर्ष-निपातेन by the falling of the rain of stones (hail), हन्यमानम् being struck, अचेतनम् bewildered (suffering), निरीक्ष्य observing, भगवान् the Supreme Lord, मेने understood, कुपित-इन्द्र-कृतम् caused by the angry Indra, हरिः Lord Hari

Anvaya: शिलावर्षनिपातेन हन्यमानम् अचेतनं निरीक्ष्य भगवान् हरिः कुपितेन्द्रकृतं मेने।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: शिलावर्षनिपातेन By the falling of the rain of stones, हन्यमानम् अचेतनं निरीक्ष्य being struck, the bewildered (creatures), observing, भगवान् हरिः the Supreme Lord Hari, कुपितेन्द्रकृतं caused by the angry Indra, मेने understood.

Simple meaning: Seeing the inhabitants of Vraja being struck and bewildered by the heavy downpour of hailstones, the Supreme Lord Hari understood that this was the work of the angry Indra.

अपर्त्त्वत्युल्बणं वर्षमतिवातं शिलामयम् ।

स्वयागे विहतेऽस्माभिरिन्द्रो नाशाय वर्षति ॥10.25.15॥

Word-by-word meaning: अप-ऋतु-अति-उल्बणम् out of season, extremely fierce, वर्षम् rain, अति-वातम् with strong winds, शिला-मयम् consisting of hailstones, स्व-यागे his own sacrifice, विहते having been frustrated, अस्माभिः by us, इन्द्रः Indra, नाशाय for destruction, वर्षति is causing rain

Anvaya: अस्माभिः स्वयागे विहते इन्द्रः अपर्त्त्वत्युल्बणम् अतिवातं शिलामयं वर्षं नाशाय वर्षति।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: अस्माभिः By us, स्वयागे विहते his own sacrifice, having been stopped, इन्द्रः Indra, अपर्त्त्वत्युल्बणम् out of season, fierce, अतिवातं शिलामयं वर्षं rains with fierce winds and hail stones नाशाय for destruction, वर्षति is raining.

Simple meaning: Krishna thought to Himself: Because we have stopped his sacrifice, Indra is now causing this extremely fierce, out-of-season rain, accompanied by terrible winds and hailstones, in order to destroy us.

तत्र प्रतिविधिं सम्यगात्मयोगेन साधये।

लोकेशमानिनां मौढ्याद्धरिष्ये श्रीमदं तमः॥10.25.16॥

Word-by-word meaning: तत्र in this matter, प्रति-विधिम् a counter-measure, सम्यक् perfectly, आत्म-योगेन by My own divine power, साधये I shall execute, लोक-ईश-मानिनाम् of those who think themselves the lords of the world, मौढ्यात् due to foolishness, हरिष्ये I shall remove, श्री-मदम् the pride of wealth, तमः the darkness

Anvaya: तत्र आत्मयोगेन सम्यक् प्रतिविधिं साधये। मौढ्यात् लोकेशमानिनां श्रीमदं तमः हरिष्ये।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: तत्र In this situation, आत्मयोगेन by My own mystic power, सम्यक् perfectly, प्रतिविधिं साधये a counter-action, I shall accomplish. मौढ्यात् Out of foolishness, लोकेशमानिनां of those who think themselves masters of the world, श्रीमदं तमः the darkness of the pride of opulence, हरिष्ये I shall remove.

Simple meaning: Krishna thought: In this situation, I shall perfectly execute a counter-measure by using My own mystic power. I will remove the dark ignorance and false pride of those who, out of foolishness, imagine themselves to be the supreme masters of the universe.

न हि सद्भावयुक्तानां सुराणामीशविस्मयः ।

मत्तोऽसतां मानभङ्गः प्रशमायोपकल्पते ॥10.25.17॥

Word-by-word meaning: न not, हि certainly, सद्भाव-युक्तानाम् who are endowed with the quality of goodness, सुराणाम् of the demigods, ईश-विस्मयः the false pride of being a lord, मत्तः from Me, असताम् of the impious, मान-भङ्गः the shattering of pride, प्रशमाय for their ultimate peace, उपकल्पते is intended

Anvaya: सद्भावयुक्तानां सुराणाम् ईशविस्मयः न हि (भवति)। मत्तः असतां मानभङ्गः प्रशमाय उपकल्पते।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: सद्भावयुक्तानां सुराणाम् Of the demigods endowed with goodness, ईशविस्मयः न हि the false pride of being a controller, does not indeed (exist). मत्तः From Me, असतां मानभङ्गः the breaking of pride of the arrogant/impious, प्रशमाय उपकल्पते is meant for their peace.

Simple meaning: Those demigods who are truly situated in the mode of goodness are never puffed up with the false pride of being supreme controllers. Therefore, when I shatter the pride of those who have become arrogant, it is actually intended for their own purification and ultimate peace.

तस्मान्मच्छरणं गोष्ठं मन्नाथं मत्परिग्रहम् ।

गोपाये स्वात्मयोगेन सोऽयं मे व्रत आहितः ॥10.25.18॥

Word-by-word meaning: तस्मात् therefore, मत्-शरणम् having taken shelter of Me, गोष्ठम् the cowherd community, मत्-नाथम् having Me as its protector, मत्-परिग्रहम् My own family, गोपाये I shall protect, स्व-आत्म्-योगेन by My own mystic power, सः अयम् this very (act), मे My, व्रतः vow, आहितः taken

Anvaya: तस्मात् मच्छरणं मन्नाथं मत्परिग्रहं गोष्ठं स्वात्मयोगेन गोपाये। सः अयं मे व्रतः आहितः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: तस्मात् Therefore, मच्छरणं which has taken shelter of Me, मन्नाथं which has Me as its lord, मत्परिग्रहं My own family/dependents, गोष्ठं the cowherd community, स्वात्मयोगेन by My own divine power, गोपाये I shall protect. सः अयं This indeed, मे व्रतः My vow, आहितः is taken.

Simple meaning: Therefore, I shall protect this cowherd community by My own mystic power. They have taken full shelter of Me, they accept Me as their only Lord, and they are My very own family. To protect them is the vow I have taken.

इत्युक्त्वैकेन हस्तेन कृत्वा गोवर्धनाचलम् ।

दधार लीलया कृष्णश्छत्राकमिव बालकः ॥10.25.19॥

Word-by-word meaning: इति having thus spoken, उक्त्वा saying, एकेन with one, हस्तेन hand, कृत्वा taking up, गोवर्धन-अचलम् the Govardhana Hill, दधार held, लीलया effortlessly (as a pastime), कृष्णः Lord Krishna, छत्राकम् a mushroom, इव like, बालकः a child

Anvaya: इति उक्त्वा कृष्णः एकेन हस्तेन गोवर्धनाचलं कृत्वा बालकः छत्राकम् इव लीलया दधार।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: इति उक्त्वा Having said this, कृष्णः Krishna, एकेन हस्तेन with one hand, गोवर्धनाचलं Govardhana Hill, कृत्वा picking up, बालकः छत्राकम् इव like a child (lifting) mushroom, लीलया दधार effortlessly held.

Simple meaning: Having said this, Lord Krishna picked up Govardhana Hill with only one hand and held it aloft as effortlessly as a child holds a mushroom.

अथाह भगवान् गोपान् हेऽम्ब तात व्रजौकसः ।

यथोपजोषं विशत गिरिगर्तं सगोधनाः ॥10.25.20॥

Word-by-word meaning: अथ then, आह addressed, भगवान् the Supreme Lord, गोपान् the cowherds, हे अम्ब O mother, तात O father, व्रज-ओकसः O inhabitants of Vraja, यथा-उपजोषम् according to your pleasure (comfortably), विशत enter, गिरि-गर्तम् the hollow space beneath the hill, स-गोधनाः along with your cows and wealth

Anvaya: अथ भगवान् गोपान् आह— हे अम्ब, तात, व्रजौकसः! सगोधनाः गिरिगर्तं यथोपजोषं विशत।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: अथ Then, भगवान् the Supreme Lord, गोपान् the cowherds, आह addressed, हे अम्ब तात व्रजौकसः O mother, O father, O residents of Vraja, सगोधनाः with your cows, गिरिगर्तं the cavity under the hill, यथोपजोषं as it pleases you (comfortably), विशत enter.

Simple meaning: Then the Supreme Lord addressed the cowherds: "O Mother, O Father, O residents of Vraja! Please enter the hollow space beneath the hill along with your cows and belongings, and make yourselves comfortable."

न त्रास इह वः कार्यो मद्धस्ताद्रिनिपातने ।

वातवर्षभयेनालं तत्त्राणं विहितं हि वः ॥10.25.21॥

Word-by-word meaning: न not, त्रासः fear, इह in this (matter), वः by you, कार्यः should be done, मत्-हस्त-अद्रि-निपातने regarding the falling of the mountain from My hand, वात-वर्ष-भयेन with the fear of wind and rain, अलम् enough, तत्-त्राणम् protection from that, विहितम् has been provided, हि certainly, वः for you

Anvaya: इह मद्धस्ताद्रिनिपातने वः त्रासः न कार्यः। वातवर्षभयेन अलम्। वः तत्त्राणं हि विहितम्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: इह In this, मद्धस्ताद्रिनिपातने regarding the falling of the mountain from My hand, वः by you, त्रासः fear, न कार्यः should not be done. वातवर्षभयेन Regarding the fear of wind and rain, अलम् enough (do not worry). वः For you, तत्त्राणं protection from that, हि विहितम् certainly has been arranged.

Simple meaning: Krishna reassured them: "You should have no fear that the mountain will fall from My hand. And do not worry any longer about the fierce wind and rain. I have already provided for your protection against them."

तथा निर्विविशुर्गर्तं कृष्णाश्वासितमानसाः ।

यथावकाशं सधनाः सव्रजाः सोपजीविनः ॥10.25.22॥

Word-by-word meaning: तथा thus, निर्विविशुः they entered, गर्तम् the hollow space (under the hill), कृष्ण-आश्वासित-मानसाः their minds being reassured by Krishna, यथा-अवकाशम् according to the available space, स-धनाः along with their wealth, स-व्रजाः with their families/community, स-उपजीविनः along with their dependents and servants

Anvaya: कृष्णाश्वासितमानसाः सधनाः सव्रजाः सोपजीविनः तथा यथावकाशं गर्तं निर्विविशुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: कृष्णाश्वासितमानसाः Those whose minds were reassured by Krishna, सधनाः with their possessions, सव्रजाः with their families, सोपजीविनः with their servants, तथा in that way, यथावकाशं according to the space available, गर्तं the cave (under the hill), निर्विविशुः entered.

Simple meaning: Reassured by Krishna's words, the inhabitants of Vraja entered the space beneath the mountain along with their families, their wealth, their servants, and their livestock, each finding a comfortable place according to the space available.

क्षुत्तृड्व्यथां सुखापेक्षां हित्वा तैर्व्रजवासिभिः ।

वीक्ष्यमाणो दधावद्रिं सप्ताहं नाचलत् पदात् ॥10.25.23॥

Word-by-word meaning: क्षुत्-तृड्-व्यथाम् the pain of hunger and thirst, सुख-अपेक्षाम् the desire for personal comfort, हित्वा casting aside, तैः by those, व्रज-वासिभिः by the residents of Vraja, वीक्ष्यमाणः being gazed upon, दधौ held, अद्रिम् the mountain, सप्त-अहम् for seven days, न not, अचलत् moved, पदात् from His place

Anvaya: तैः व्रजवासिभिः वीक्ष्यमाणः (सः) क्षुत्तृड्व्यथां सुखापेक्षां हित्वा सप्ताहम् अद्रिं दधौ, पदात् न अचलत् च।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: तैः व्रजवासिभिः By those residents of Vraja, वीक्ष्यमाणः being looked at, क्षुत्तृड्व्यथां सुखापेक्षां the pain of hunger and thirst and the desire for comfort, हित्वा having abandoned, सप्ताहम् for a week, अद्रिं दधौ held the mountain, च and, पदात् न अचलत् did not move from His spot.

Simple meaning: While the residents of Vraja gazed at Him, Lord Krishna disregarded hunger, thirst, and personal comfort. He held the mountain aloft for seven days straight, without moving a single step from His position.

कृष्णयोगानुभावं तं निशाम्येन्द्रोऽतिविस्मितः ।

निःस्तम्भो भ्रष्टसङ्कल्पः स्वान् मेघान् संन्यवारयत् ॥10.25.24॥

Word-by-word meaning: कृष्ण-योग-अनुभावम् the power of Krishna’s mystic union (potency), तम् that, निशाम्य observing/seeing, इन्द्रः Indra, अति-विस्मितः greatly amazed, निःस्तम्भः his pride shattered (freed from arrogance), भ्रष्ट-सङ्कल्पः his determination frustrated, स्वान् his own, मेघान् clouds, संन्यवारयत् restrained/called back

Anvaya: तं कृष्णयोगानुभावं निशाम्य अतिविस्मितः निःस्तम्भः भ्रष्टसङ्कल्पः इन्द्रः स्वान् मेघान् संन्यवारयत्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: तं कृष्णयोगानुभावं That power of Krishna's yoga, निशाम्य having seen, अतिविस्मितः greatly astonished, निःस्तम्भः being devoid of arrogance, भ्रष्टसङ्कल्पः whose resolve was broken, इन्द्रः Indra, स्वान् मेघान् his own clouds, संन्यवारयत् stopped/recalled.

Simple meaning: Upon witnessing Krishna's extraordinary mystic power, Indra was utterly amazed. His pride was shattered, and his plan to destroy the cowherds was completely frustrated. He then ordered his clouds to stop their downpour and withdraw.

खं व्यभ्रमुदितादित्यं वातवर्षं च दारुणम् ।

निशाम्योपरतं गोपान् गोवर्धनधरोऽब्रवीत् ॥10.25.25॥

Word-by-word meaning: खम् the sky, वि-अभ्रम् free of clouds, उदित-आदित्यम् with the sun having risen, वात-वर्षम् the wind and rain, च and, दारुणम् fierce, निशाम्य observing, उपरतम् having ceased, गोपान् to the cowherds, गोवर्धन-धरः the lifter of Govardhana (Krishna), अब्रवीत् spoke

Anvaya: व्यभ्रं खं उदितादित्यं दारुणं वातवर्षं च उपरतं निशाम्य गोवर्धनधरः गोपान् अब्रवीत्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: व्यभ्रं खम् Cloudless sky, उदितादित्यं the sun risen, दारुणं the fierce, वातवर्षं wind and rain, च and, उपरतं ceased, निशाम्य having observed, गोवर्धनधरः the upholder of Govardhana, गोपान् अब्रवीत् spoke to the cowherds.

Simple meaning: Seeing that the sky was now clear of clouds, the sun had risen, and the fierce wind and rain had completely stopped, Lord Krishna, the lifter of Govardhana Hill, addressed the cowherds.

निर्यात त्यजत त्रासं गोपाः सस्त्रीधनार्भकाः ।

उपारतं वातवर्षं व्युदप्रायाश्च निम्नगाः ॥10.25.26॥

Word-by-word meaning: निर्यात go out, त्यजत abandon, त्रासम् fear, गोपाः O cowherd men, स-स्त्री-धन-अर्भकाः along with your women, wealth, and children, उपारतम् has ceased, वात-वर्षम् the wind and rain, वि-उद​-प्रायाः mostly receded (the water level has gone down), च and, निम्नगाः the rivers

Anvaya: हे गोपाः! सस्त्रीधनार्भकाः (यूयम्) निर्यात। त्रासं त्यजत। वातवर्षम् उपारतम्। निम्नगाः च व्युदप्रायाः (सन्ति)।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: हे गोपाः O cowherds, सस्त्रीधनार्भकाः with your women, wealth, and children, निर्यात go out. त्रासं त्यजत Abandon fear. वातवर्षम् उपारतम् The wind and rain has stopped. निम्नगाः च And the rivers, व्युदप्रायाः have mostly drained of their excess water.

Simple meaning: Krishna said: "O cowherds, please go out now with your wives, children, and cattle, and leave all fear behind. The wind and rain have stopped, and the flooded rivers have receded."

ततस्ते निर्ययुर्गोपाः स्वं स्वमादाय गोधनम् ।

शकटोढोपकरणं स्त्रीबालस्थविराः शनैः ॥10.25.27॥

Word-by-word meaning: ततः then, ते they, निर्ययुः went out, गोपाः the cowherds, स्वम् स्वम् each their own, आदाय taking, गो-धनम् wealth of cows, शकट-ऊढ-उपकरणम् the equipment carried on wagons, स्त्री-बाल-स्थविराः the women, children, and the elderly, शनैः slowly

Anvaya: ततः ते गोपाः स्त्रीबालस्थविराः स्वं स्वं गोधनं शकटोढोपकरणं (च) आदाय शनैः निर्ययुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: ततः Then, ते गोपाः those cowherds, स्त्रीबालस्थविराः (along with) the women, children and elderly, स्वं स्वं each their own, गोधनं wealth of cattle, च and, शकटोढोपकरणं possessions loaded on wagons, आदाय having taken, शनैः निर्ययुः slowly went out.

Simple meaning: Then those cowherds, along with the women, children, and elderly, slowly departed from beneath the hill, taking with them their respective herds of cattle and all their belongings loaded on wagons.

भगवानपि तं शैलं स्वस्थाने पूर्ववत् प्रभुः ।

पश्यतां सर्वभूतानां स्थापयामास लीलया ॥10.25.28॥

Word-by-word meaning: भगवान् the Supreme Lord, अपि also, तम् that, शैलम् mountain, स्व-स्थाने in its own place, पूर्व-वत् as before, प्रभुः the Almighty, पश्यताम् while watching, सर्व-भूतानाम् all living beings, स्थापयामास placed/set down, लीलया effortlessly (as a pastime)

Anvaya: प्रभुः भगवान् अपि सर्वभूतानां पश्यतां तं शैलं पूर्ववत् स्वस्थाने लीलया स्थापयामास।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: प्रभुः भगवान् अपि The Almighty Lord (Krishna) also, सर्वभूतानां पश्यतां while all living beings were watching, तं शैलं that mountain, पूर्ववत् as it was before, स्वस्थाने in its original place, लीलया effortlessly, स्थापयामास placed.

Simple meaning: Then the Almighty Lord Krishna, while all living beings looked on, effortlessly placed the mountain back in its original position, exactly as it had been before.

तं प्रेमवेगान्निभृता व्रजौकसो यथा समीयुः परिरम्भणादिभिः ।

गोप्यश्च सस्नेहमपूजयन् मुदा दध्यक्षताद्भिर्युयुजुः सदाशिषः ॥10.25.29॥

Word-by-word meaning: तम् Him (Krishna), प्रेम-वेगात्-निभृताः overwhelmed by the force of their love, व्रज-ओकसः the inhabitants of Vraja, यथा as (befitting their relationship), समीयुः approached, परिरम्भण-आदिभिः with embracing and other gestures, गोप्यः the cowherd women (Gopis), च and, स-स्नेहम् with affection, अपूजयन् worshipped, मुदा joyfully, दधि-अक्षत-अद्भिः with yogurt, unbroken parched rice, and water, युयुजुः bestowed, सत्-आशिषः sincere blessings

Anvaya: प्रेमवेगान्निभृताः व्रजौकसः परिरम्भणादिभिः यथा (योग्यम्) तं समीयुः। गोप्यः च मुदा सस्नेहं (तम्) अपूजयन् दध्यक्षताद्भिः सदाशिषः युयुजुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: प्रेमवेगान्निभृताः Overwhelmed by the force of love, व्रजौकसः the residents of Vraja, परिरम्भणादिभिः through embracing and so on, यथा as was appropriate, तं समीयुः approached Him. गोप्यः च And the Gopis, मुदा joyfully, सस्नेहं with affection, अपूजयन् worshipped (Him), दध्यक्षताद्भिः with yogurt and parched rice, सदाशिषः युयुजुः and bestowed best blessings.

Simple meaning: Overwhelmed by a surge of pure love, the residents of Vraja approached Krishna, greeting Him with embraces and other expressions of affection according to their specific relationships. The Gopis joyfully worshipped Him with offerings of yogurt, parched rice, and water, while bestowing their heartfelt blessings upon Him.

यशोदा रोहिणी नन्दो रामश्च बलिनां वरः ।

कृष्णमालिङ्ग्य युयुजुराशिषः स्नेहकातराः ॥10.25.30॥

Word-by-word meaning: यशोदा Yashoda, रोहिणी Rohini, नन्दः Nanda, रामः Rama, च and, बलिनाम् of the strong, वरः the best, कृष्णम् Krishna, आलिङ्ग्य embracing, युयुजुः offered/bestowed, आशिषः blessings, स्नेह-कातराः overcome with affection

Anvaya: यशोदा रोहिणी नन्दः बलिनां वरः रामः च स्नेहकातराः कृष्णम् आलिङ्ग्य आशिषः युयुजुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: यशोदा रोहिणी नन्दः Yashoda, Rohini, Nanda, बलिनां वरः रामः च and the best among the strong, Rama, स्नेहकातराः overcome with affection, कृष्णम् आलिङ्ग्य having embraced Krishna, आशिषः युयुजुः gave blessings.

Simple meaning: Yashoda, Rohini, Nanda, and Balarama - the greatest of the strong were all overwhelmed with deep affection. They embraced Krishna and showered Him with their most heartfelt blessings.

दिवि देवगणाः साध्याः सिद्धगन्धर्वचारणाः ।

तुष्टुवुर्मुमुचुस्तुष्टाः पुष्पवर्षाणि पार्थिव ॥10.25.31॥

Word-by-word meaning: दिवि in the sky, देव-गणाः the various groups of demigods, साध्याः the Sadhyas, सिद्ध-गन्धर्व-चारणाः the Siddhas, Gandharvas, and Charanas, तुष्टुवुः offered prayers, मुमुचुः released, तुष्टाः being satisfied/pleased, पुष्प-वर्षाणि rains of flowers, पार्थिव O King (Parikshit)

Anvaya: पार्थिव! दिवि देवगणाः साध्याः सिद्धगन्धर्वचारणाः तुष्टाः (सन्तः) तुष्टुवुः पुष्पवर्षाणि मुमुचुः च।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: पार्थिव O King, दिवि in the heavens, देवगणाः the hosts of demigods, साध्याः the Sadhyas, सिद्धगन्धर्वचारणाः the Siddhas, Gandharvas, and Charanas, तुष्टाः being highly pleased, तुष्टुवुः praised (Krishna), च and, पुष्पवर्षाणि showers of flowers, मुमुचुः released.

Simple meaning: O King Parikshit, in the sky, the demigods—including the Sadhyas, Siddhas, Gandharvas, and Charanas—were all greatly satisfied. They sang the praises of Lord Krishna and showered down rains of flowers.

शङ्खदुन्दुभयो नेदुर्दिवि देवप्रणोदिताः ।

जगुर्गन्धर्वपतयस्तुम्बुरुप्रमुखा नृप ॥10.25.32॥

Word-by-word meaning: शङ्ख-दुन्दुभयः conch shells and kettledrums, नेदुः resounded, दिवि in the sky, देव-प्रणोदिताः sounded by the demigods, जगुः sang, गन्धर्व-पतयः the chiefs of the Gandharvas, तुम्बुरु-प्रमुखाः led by Tumburu, नृप O King

Anvaya: नृप! दिवि देवप्रणोदिताः शङ्खदुन्दुभयः नेदुः। तुम्बुरुप्रमुखाः गन्धर्वपतयः जगुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: नृप O King, दिवि in the heavens, देवप्रणोदिताः played by the demigods, शङ्खदुन्दुभयः नेदुः conch shells and kettledrums, sounded. तुम्बुरुप्रमुखाः Headed by Tumburu, गन्धर्वपतयः the lords of the Gandharvas, जगुः sang.

Simple meaning: O King, in the celestial sky, conchshells and kettledrums resounded as they were played by the demigods. Meanwhile, the leading Gandharvas, headed by Tumburu, sang the glories of Lord Krishna.

ततोऽनुरक्तैः पशुपैः परिश्रितो राजन् स गोष्ठं सबलोऽव्रजद्धरिः ।

तथाविधान्यस्य कृतानि गोपिका गायन्त्य ईयुर्मुदिता हृदिस्पृशः ॥10.25.33॥

Word-by-word meaning: ततः then, अनुरक्तैः by the loving, पशु-पैः cowherds, परिश्रितः surrounded, राजन् O King, सः He (Krishna), गोष्ठम् to the cowherd village, स-बलः along with Balarama, अव्रजत् went, हरिः Lord Hari, तथा-विधानि of that nature (extraordinary), अस्य His, कृतानि deeds, गोपिकाः the cowherd women, गायन्त्यः singing, ईयुः went, मुदिताः joyfully, हृदि-स्पृशः touching the heart

Anvaya: राजन्! ततः सबलः सः हरिः अनुरक्तैः पशुपैः परिश्रितः गोष्ठम् अव्रजत्। मुदिताः गोपिकाः अस्य तथाविधानि हृदिस्पृशः कृतानि गायन्त्यः ईयुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: राजन् O King, ततः Then, सबलः with Balarama, सः हरिः that Lord Hari, अनुरक्तैः पशुपैः by the devoted cowherds, परिश्रितः surrounded, गोष्ठम् अव्रजत् to the village, went. मुदिताः गोपिकाः The joyful Gopis, अस्य His, तथाविधानि of such a kind, हृदिस्पृशः कृतानि heart-touching pastimes, गायन्त्यः ईयुः singing, went.

Simple meaning: O King Parikshit, thereafter Lord Hari, accompanied by Balarama and surrounded by the devoted cowherds, returned to the village of Vraja. The Gopis, their hearts full of joy, sang of the wonderful, heart-touching pastimes the Lord had performed.

इति श्रीमद्भागवते महापुराणे पारमहंस्यां संहितायां दशमस्कन्धे पूर्वार्धे “गोवर्धनधारणलीला नाम” पञ्चविंशः अध्यायः ॥25॥

Thus ends the twenty-fifth discourse named “The Lord lifts up Mount Govardhana” in the first half of Book Ten of the great and glorious Bhagavata-Purana.
